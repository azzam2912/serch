the insurgents elected a number of city elders and put a metropolitan clerk named ivan zheglov in charge of municipal government on march 17 metropolitan nikon of novgorod anathemized the new municipal authority from a church ambon for which he would be beaten up by the crowd on march 19 tsar alexei mikhailovich dispatched a noble named solovtsov to settle the conflict but the latter was arrested on the spot and kept under guard for several days the insurgents attempted to establish contact with rebellious pskov see pskov uprising of 1650 but to no avail eventually the internal struggle for power between the urban poor and well off citizens ivan zheglov s hesitation and inconsistency and metropolitan nikon s firm stance led to the insurgents defeat the government troops under the command of prince ivan nikitich khovansky approached novgorod in early april and after having spent a few days at the city walls entered the city on april 13 meeting no resistance the leaders of the uprising were arrested five of whom would soon be executed over a hundred people were flogged and exiled to the russian north astrakhan and terek
