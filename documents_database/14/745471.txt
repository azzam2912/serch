born in london the son of solicitor dingwall latham bateson and the great nephew of rugby player harold dingwall bateson he was educated at uppingham school in rutland and wadham college oxford at oxford he read history rowed cox for the wadham college boat club during eights week and performed in the oxford university dramatic society bateson s stage credits included the first british production of samuel beckett s waiting for godot in 1955 at the arts theatre in london in a production directed by peter hall he appeared in many film television and radio productions including the cadfael chronicles doctor who in the serial entitled the ribos operation and labyrinth he also provided the voices for several characters in the children s tv series tugs 1988 since 1994 he did the voice of measley from the audiotape version of the animals of farthing wood bateson was featured in many productions of focus on the family radio theater among his appearances on the program were in a christmas carol 1996 where he doubled as narrator and as the ghost of christmas present dietrich bonhoeffer the cost of freedom 1997 portraying dr karl bonhoeffer ben hur 2001 portraying balthasar and father
