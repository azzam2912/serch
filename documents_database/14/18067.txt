900 1168 ce the later aztec culture saw the toltecs as their intellectual and cultural predecessors and described toltec culture emanating from t ll n nahuatl for tula as the epitome of civilization in the nahuatl language the word t lt catl singular or t lt cah plural came to take on the meaning artisan the aztec oral and pictographic tradition also described the history of the toltec empire giving lists of rulers and their exploits modern scholars debate whether the aztec narratives of toltec history should be given credence as descriptions of actual historical events while all scholars acknowledge that there is a large mythological part of the narrative some maintain that by using a critical comparative method some level of historicity can be salvaged from the sources others maintain that continued analysis of the narratives as sources of actual history is futile and hinders access to actual knowledge of the culture of tula de allende other controversies relating to the toltecs include the question of how best to understand the reasons behind the perceived similarities in architecture and iconography between the archaeological site of tula and the maya site of chich n itz no consensus has yet emerged
