this book contains the prophecies attributed to the prophet ezekiel and is one of the books of the prophets the original text of this chapter is written in the hebrew language this chapter is divided into 24 verses some early manuscripts containing the text of this chapter in hebrew are of the masoretic text tradition which includes the codex cairensis 895 the petersburg codex of the prophets 916 aleppo codex 10th century codex leningradensis 1008 there is also a translation into koine greek known as the septuagint made in the last few centuries bc extant ancient manuscripts of the septuagint version include codex vaticanus b formula 1 4th century codex sinaiticus s bhk formula 1 4th century codex alexandrinus a formula 1 5th century and codex marchalianus q formula 1 6th century this messianic allegory is presented with the reference to the branch in which grows to be a majestic cedar as shown in the daily mass readings provided in the latin rite of the roman catholic church one of the main references in the gospels is the parable of the mustard seed
