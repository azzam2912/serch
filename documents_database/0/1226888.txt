dampier is usually considered the main administrative locality for the shelf the production areas are located offshore and within the jurisdiction of the western australian state government the two main production areas are the thevenard production area close to onslow and the varanus production area west of dampier the north west shelf oil extraction is negligible compared to the volume of gas produced the north west shelf venture is a project to extract resources within the region from various gas fields however it is considered to be only in part of the whole shelf region it involves developments on the burrup peninsula barrow island and other locations considerable parts of the region are the highest prospective gas and oil areas of australia the main sedimentary basin providing the opportunity is the northern carnarvon basin however it is only one part of the regional complex
