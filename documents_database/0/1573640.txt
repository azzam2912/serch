the main section runs from gothenburg central station to kil station mostly along the west shore of v nern there is a branch from erikstad to the norway sweden border at kornsj from where it continues as the stfold line to oslo the lengths of the line are from gothenburg to erikstad from gothenburg to kornsj and from gothenburg to kil the line is single track except for the section from gothenburg to xnered which is double track as of 2018 the following passenger rail services exist on the line in addition freight trains operate on the entire railway from gothenburg to falun and to places in northern sweden via kil and between gothenburg and norway via kornsj the railways were finished in 1879 a shortcut at erikstad was built for the norway branch in 1995 the section from gothenburg to xnered was rebuilt as a new double track railway in the period 2005 2012 the current name was introduced in 1990 from the construction until then the name was bergslagen line between gothenburg through kil to falun later g vle the line to norway then started at mellerud and was called dalsland line
