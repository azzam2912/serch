the album entered the uk albums chart at no 1 upon its opening week and spent a total of 49 weeks on the chart a new day at midnight produced two singles the other side in december 2002 and be mine in april 2003 dead in the water was released as a promo only single in november 2002 both commercial singles failed to break into the top 20 of the uk singles chart peaking at no 35 and no 23 respectively the album received critically mixed reviews but went on to be certified platinum four times the album is dedicated to david gray s father peter who died of cancer in 2001 at the height of gray s popularity a song entitled a new day at midnight written about the birth of his first daughter ivy was written during the album s recording sessions but was not included on the album
