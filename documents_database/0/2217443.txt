the prix d t was one of the top harness races in north america until 1992 the 1993 edition had to be cancelled due to a five month strike action by horsemen however the race was not held again before being revived in 2014 at hippodrome 3r in trois rivi res as a race for four year olds with a purse of 500 000 in 2019 it is currently the most remunerative race in north america for four year old pacers this event was held for the first time in 1966 under the name of prix d automne autumn classic at the now defunct blue bonnets raceway in montreal run over a distance of one mile on a 5 8 mile oval track the race was open to pacers age four and older a 50 000 purse made it the richest harness race at that time in canadian history in 1967 the race was renamed the l amble du centenaire centennial pace in honor of canada s 100th anniversary and made open to pacers age three and older in 1968 it became the prix d t and in 1971 was modified to a stake race for three year old pacers
