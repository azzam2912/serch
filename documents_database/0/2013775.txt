she was born in caernarvon north wales she was educated at grammar school and attended the university college of north wales bangor where she obtained a bachelor of arts degree and a diploma of education for some time she taught disabled children and then took up writing she has produced many books and contributed short stories to many magazines peters is also known as a bronte scholar maureen peters was married and divorced twice she had two sons and two daughters she died on 8 april 2008 her novels have often focused on royalty mostly the war of the roses and tudor period and cover the lives of elizabeth i of england anne boleyn catherine howard mary tudor queen of france as well as of other famous and less famous historical figures such as edward ii of england the many queen consorts of various kings of england apart from biographical fiction on royalty written under her own name she also wrote gothic romances family sagas mills boon series titles contemporary mysteries malone trilogy the vinegar trilogy
