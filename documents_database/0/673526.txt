the vast majority of fax machines have an adf allowing the unattended sending of multi page faxes due to the ubiquity of adf in fax machines some fax machine owners use the fax machine as a scanner faxing multi page documents to themselves document feeders are described by speed in pages per minute or ppm and capacity usually in a range from 10 sheets to 200 there are two kinds of document feeders capable of two sided duplex scanning a reversing automatic document feeder or radf scans one side of a page then flips it and scans the other side a duplexing automatic document feeder or dadf scans both sides in one pass the advantage of the dadf is faster speed for two sided originals radfs and dadfs are rated in images per minute ipm the number of sides they can scan each minute this may depend upon the resolution being used rather than the maximum resolution supported
