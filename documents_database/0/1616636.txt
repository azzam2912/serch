the station is part of the north island line nil proposal which includes the extension of the tseung kwan o line from its present north point terminus to tamar station in central the kcrc also proposed adding causeway bay north station as part of the sha tin to central link scl to be situated between exhibition station and hung hom station however a major drawback was that road traffic would be severely impacted as this would involve closing gloucester road for 5 years eventually this station was eliminated from the scl plan altogether after the mtr kcr merger and will only be part of the nil if it is constructed in 2013 the name causeway bay north was mentioned in the review and update of the railway development strategy 2000 as an intermediate station on the north island line instead of victoria park
