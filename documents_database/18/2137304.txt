since its inception american girl has published books based on the dolls with novels and other media to tie in with their dolls the books follow various american girls throughout both historical eras and contemporary settings the historical novels that have corresponding dolls are referred to as the central series there is also the girl of the year line of characters from contemporary settings a related series entitled history mysteries also known as mysteries through time and or mysteries through history was released by american girl in 1999 and discontinued in 2004 the series comprises a total of 22 books by various authors and forms a companion series to the popular american girl books unlike girl of the year and other lines they do not come with any doll or toy and acts as a stand alone novel set in a particular period in american history the characters were generally older ranging from ages ten to fourteen and the books were marketed as for ages ten and up many of the authors from this series later moved on to write for the central series several books from the series were later re released in digital form by open road integrated
