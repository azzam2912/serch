every episode the beerists taste and review a panel of craft beers ending each episode with an individual rating of each beer some episodes include guest tasters the podcast launched in may 2012 created by john rubio and includes the other three beerists anastacia kelly grant davis and mike lambert anastacia kelly has since left the podcast and the group now includes laura christie in 2016 the beerists podcast won in the category of arts for the people s choice podcast awards john rubio is the host and show runner of the beerists podcast a professional graphic designer he began his journey into craft beer in 1994 when he left home to attend university in central texas he currently works with several breweries on branding and design grant davis was a beer novice when started his tenure with the beerists podcast grant hosts and appears on a multitude of other podcasts like the tv dudes and star trek discovery pod and co runs a podcast recording studio in austin tx mike lambert has organized a monthly beer tasting in austin for over 10 years and currently works at a brewery in austin tx mike has been on the beerists podcast
