she was launched on 5 december 1964 sponsored by mrs francis l moseley and mrs leon v chaplin and commissioned on 22 october 1965 with captain donald m miller commanding the blue crew and commander ross n williams commanding the gold crew on 6 december 1965 the gold crew successfully launched a polaris a 3 ballistic missile in close coordination with an orbital pass of the gemini 7 astronauts frank borman and jim lovell benjamin franklin was decommissioned on 23 november 1993 and stricken from the naval vessel register the same day her scrapping via the nuclear powered ship and submarine recycling program in bremerton washington was completed on 21 august 1995
