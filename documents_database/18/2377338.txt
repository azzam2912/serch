three concerts are performed every season accompanied by professional orchestras and soloists founded in 1994 by a group of non professional singers under the direction of edward j tipton the pcs quickly became known for its high standard of performance and its annual sing along of handel s messiah which has been held every december since the inception of the choir the current music director is zachary ullery in recent years the paris choral society has performed orff s carmina burana june 2017 mozart s great mass in c minor 2016 handel s dixit dominus and coronation anthems november 2015 beethoven s missa solemnis in the church of saint eustache march 2015 faur s requiem and bernstein s chichester psalms november 2014 rachmaninoff s vespers march 2017 bach s b minor mass april 2014 brahms a german requiem haydn s the creation 2013 mozart s requiem 2011 2018 bach s st john passion 2012 morten lauridsen s lux aeterna 2011 mendelssohn s elijah 2010 and rossini s petite messe solonelle 2011
