she had one sister elaine brogdon 1934 2009 she came of age during the depression in a cold water flat in brooklyn she began to study art at the brooklyn museum when she was eight years old primarily as a means to stay warm in the new york winter the wpa was one of many programs designed to counter the ravages of the depression in the thirties and sponsored classes at the museum at first mara was deemed to young to participate but was nevertheless allowed to attend classes her focus quickly went in the direction of painting though and she began to develop a vocabulary that lent itself to abstract art a short year later at the age of nine mara had her first show at the brooklyn museum as an abstract artist mara devereux came to obtain a place of respect and prominence she lived around and with many prominent people having at one time been married to a united states representative to nato and following that to pop artist robert dowd a musician as well as an artist she played the bodhran a celtic hand drum in an irish ensemble that opened once for the grateful dead
