the building was listed on the danish registry of protected buildings and places in 1950 otto frello who owned it from 1966 until his death has painted a trompe l oeil mural of a door on the first floor the building was constructed in 1797 for tea merchant knud steenberg after the previous building at the site had been destroyed in the copenhagen fire of 1795 stenberg ran a tea store from the high celler the building was refurbished in 1960 under supervision of the architect svend lindholm born 1917 the artist otto frello purchased the property in 1966 and members of his family owned and lived in it until 2016 the new owner ejendomsselskabet v s larsen a s a property company carried out a comprehensive restoration of the building in 2018 it was sold for dkk 32 5 the building consists of three floors over a high celler it has six bays towards brol gggerstr de a corner bay and five bays towards badstuestr de the building has a total floor area of 622 square metres many original doors panels floors and other details in the building have been preserved the first floor features a trompe l oeil
