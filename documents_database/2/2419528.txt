in the early 1860s billing enrolled as a theology student at lund university billing represented a very conservative political view that has sometimes been called oscarian after king oscar ii in 1881 he became professor of practical theology at lund university in 1884 he was appointed bishop of v ster s and was consecrated on september 7 1884 by archbishop anton niklas sundberg in v ster s he deepened his contacts with the royal family he also represented v ster s in the parliament s first chamber on the conservative bench in 1900 after the death of anton niklas sundberg billing was offered the archbishopric of uppsala however he refused nonetheless he accepted to take over sundberg s place in the swedish academy a post he kept until his death in 1925 in 1898 he was appointed bishop of lund billing was elected as a member of the royal swedish academy of sciences in 1908 and the royal society of the humanities at lund in 1919 he was awarded an honorary doctorate in lund on september 28 1918 and received the royal order of the seraphim on june 6 1921 in 1868 he married frida bring daughter of bishop ebbe
