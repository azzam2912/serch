cameron studied at saint francis xavier college and in 1861 graduated in medicine from the university of pennsylvania and also from the school of practical obstetrics in philadelphia he practiced medicine at mabou in cape breton he studied further at bellevue hospital medical school in new york city from 1864 to 1865 before returning to his practice cameron also served as surgeon for the militia he was elected as a member of the anti confederation party to the 1st canadian parliament on september 20 1867 he became a member of the liberal conservative party on january 30 1869 though he was defeated in the following three elections he was re elected to the 5th canadian parliament on june 20 1882 and to the two following parliaments he became a member of the conservative party on april 13 1887 cameron was appointed in the legislative council of nova scotia in 1879 serving until 1882
