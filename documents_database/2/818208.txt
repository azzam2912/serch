it s the first lighthouse built in puerto rico the first castillo san felipe del morro lighthouse was built in 1846 and exhibited a light using five parabolic reflectors in 1876 a new octagonal iron tower was constructed atop the walls of the fort the tower was hit by u s artillery fire in the puerto rican campaign of the spanish american war on may 12 1898 the lighthouse was rebuilt in 1899 but developed structural problems and was demolished in 1906 the new and current lighthouse was constructed in 1908 as a moorish revival style square tower on castle public admission tours into the tower are held and the castillo san felipe del morro along with castillo san crist bal and much of the city walls are part of the san juan national historic site also open to the public
