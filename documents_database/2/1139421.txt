in terms of record sales the highpoint of his career was reached when his single i don t want to put a hold on you rose over a period of six weeks to number three in the uk singles chart in the week ending 23 april 1977 further success came in the form of another top 75 hit southern comfort and one gold and two silver selling albums in the late 1970s flint presented the itv show pop gospel in 1985 flint was one of the co presenters on the itv television programme mooncat co although no longer a commercial recording artist flint continues to perform regularly and is active on the after dinner circuit his song i don t want to put a hold on you appeared on the 2008 compilation album 70s dinner party
