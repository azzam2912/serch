the device is essentially two pieces of metal tube bent in a loop and connected with a hinge the intended use is to put the thighmaster between the knees and squeeze them together this exercises the hip adductors the simple mechanism allows exercising any suitable muscle where a small angle can be created to press it for example the biceps elbow flexion or the hamstrings knee flexion as with most fitness products marketed to homes advertisements often emphasized that the thighmaster could be used while doing something else the infomercials featured people watching television and exercising with the thighmaster at the same time it was mainly advertised in the 1990s by the actress suzanne somers the thighmaster was invented in sweden by then physical medicine intern later dr anne marie bennstrom prescott as the v bar physical therapy device it later received us design patent number 343882s as physical exerciser it achieved commercial success when marketed under the thighmaster name by joshua reynolds often erroneously credited as the inventor who also made a great deal of money with his version of the mood ring reynolds is an heir to the fortune of r j reynolds founder of r j reynolds
