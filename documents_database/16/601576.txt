the ruins of the site and an archaeological museum are located south of the modern town scoglitti a frazione of the comune vittoria in the province of ragusa it was founded by syracuse in 599 bc but destroyed by the mother city in 552 bc its remains are today in the municipality of ragusa the geloans however founded it anew in 461 bc under the olympic charioteer psaumis of camarina it seems to have been in general hostile to syracuse but though an ally of athens in 427 bc it gave some slight help to syracuse in 415 413 bc it was destroyed by the carthaginians in 405 bc restored by timoleon in 339 bc after its abandonment by dionysius order but in 258 bc fell into the hands of the romans its complete destruction dates from ad 853 the site of the ancient city is among rapidly shifting sandhills and the lack of stone in the neighborhood has led to its buildings being used as a quarry even by the inhabitants of gela so that nothing is now visible above ground but a small part of the wall of the temple of athena and a few foundations of houses
