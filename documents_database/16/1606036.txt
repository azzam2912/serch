only local services on the chikuhi line stop at this station the station consists of an island platforms serving two tracks with a siding the station building is a modern structure of steel and plate glass and is unstaffed serving only as a waiting room access to the island platform is by means of a footbridge the private kitakyushu railway opened a track between and on 5 december 1923 with shikaka opening on the same day as an intermediate station between the two when the kitakyushu railway was nationalized on 1 october 1937 japanese government railways jgr took over control of the station and designated the line which served it as the chikuhi line with the privatization of japanese national railways jnr the successor of jgr on 1 april 1987 control of the station passed to jr kyushu in 2012 work began on a new station building to replace the old one which had been built in 1938 the old station building was demolished thereafter in fiscal 2012 there were a daily average of 78 passengers boarding only using the station
