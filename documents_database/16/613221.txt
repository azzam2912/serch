due to financial difficulties the zoo decided in 2011 to reorganise it closed at the end of 2015 but reopened early 2016 on another location as wildlands adventure zoo emmen built in 1935 and completely renewed in 1970 dierenpark emmen was considered to be one of the finest zoos in europe and has won various prizes animals live in carefully reconstructed natural habitats according to vegetation climate and elevation because of lack of space a new second site was opened in 1998 just outside emmen the two parts of the zoo were connected with each other and one ticket gave admission to both parts the zoo has had great success breeding asian elephants with 26 births since 1992 and four births in 2010 and 2011 alone in 2011 there were 13 elephants at the zoo others having been transported to other zoos the elephant accommodations comprise a large moated island for females and calves and a separate smaller island for the bull with indoor housing being underground the city council of emmen approved the construction of a completely new zoo the wildlands which is expected to draw 1 3 million visitors annually the new zoo was completed in march 2016
