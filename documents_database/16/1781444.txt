irenopolis was also an episcopal see that is now included in the catholic church s list of titular sees it is located on the site of d zi i formerly haruniye in the province of osmaniye located at the mouth of the darb al ain pass which led from al h r n ya to germanikeia 104 km se of adana and 29 5 km ne of osmaniye e honigmann s identification of irenopolis with bagdacik the little irenopolis in contrast to the great irenopolis 25 km ne of osmaniye on the way to hasanbeyli is wrong because the area is called bu daycik the site is a little higher than hierapolis kastabala and anazarbus on the mountain preventing access to the cilician plain from the east it was probably founded by antiochus iv of commagene the coins of the city show that asclepius and hygeia were worshiped in the city during pagan era the cult of the two gods in the city may be connected with the natural spring of the area theodoret of cyrus confirms that the two names eirenopolis and neronias apparently refer to the same city the name neronias refereeing to honors bestowed on the city
