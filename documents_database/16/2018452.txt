its lineup consists of nina donghia vocals milan mcalevey guitar and vocals caleb davis guitar zach soares bass guitar and peter cuffari drums lead vocal duties are shared between nina and milan the band s unique blend of indie and psychedelic rock is strongly influenced by bands of the 1960s and described as slow and dreamy psych rock after forming the lil fighters with walter martin in the late 90s mcalevey recorded several albums at the walkmen s original marcata recording studio in harlem eventually he left the band and new york for maine and teamed up with donghia in bar harbor on mount desert island to form coke weed the band spent the fall of 2010 recording their debut album coke weed volume one which was released the following year instead of promoting or touring volume one the group got back to work recording their sophomore album nice dreams which was released in the spring of 2012 the band toured the east coast heavily in the spring and summer of 2012 supporting the walkmen and woods the band spent the first half of 2013 playing shows mostly in their home state of maine and working on their third lp
