the club became two times champion of lw w district league liga okr gowa where it spent 17 seasons after invasion of poland by the soviet forces in 1939 the club was dissolved and its place was formed fc lokomotyv lviv the club s name comes from lechia the original name for poland the club s tradition as a polish club was continued by people expelled from lw w who moved to gda sk and created lechia gdansk after war in 1945 such as ryszard koncewicz in 2019 the lechia lw w team had been reconstructed by the historical reconstruction association in poland as part of the retro liga project the players play in carefully recreated costumes and shoes according to the rules of 1938 the teams chosen for the retro liga were those forced to disband in 1939 after the outbreak of world war ii the other teams who took part were wks kutno wks owicz wks grodno grodno were not reformed as the borders after wwii meant they were now in belorussian territory and mig y wilno mig y were not reformed as the borders after wwii meant they were now in lithuanian territory
