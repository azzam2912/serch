he was the son of an army surgeon henry john waller barrow and florence ellie macdonald barrow was educated at bedford modern school and guy s hospital london barrow joined the royal army medical corps from guy s in 1898 he served in the south african war world war i and the third anglo afghan war in 1919 in 1919 he was appointed director of hygiene and pathology in india and later director of hygiene at the war office between 1924 and 1930 in 1926 he was appointed an honorary surgeon to king george v after his retirement he was sent to antigua as health officer and he was a member of the federal executive council of the leeward islands from 1930 until 1933 from 1941 to 1946 he served as colonel commandant royal army medical corps and from 1943 to 1952 he was a commissioner of the royal hospital chelsea barrow died in hampshire england on 20 december 1957
