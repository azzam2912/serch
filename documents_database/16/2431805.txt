the four teams will start in different corners of the earth and all race along designated routes to a buoy in the middle of the ocean designating the center of the earth there will be no eliminations but the first team to reach the buoy wins and they get to split the million dollar prize that goes along with it geoff daniels executive vp of unscripted entertainment for national geographic networks stated unlike other competition formats race to the center of the earth will combine the grittiness of a survival show with the cinematic style of a feature film action thriller dropping viewers into the middle of a heart pounding journey unlike anything ever made for television race to the center of the earth is expected to begin production later this year possibly between july 25 and october 30 2019 casting has officially begun by raph korine and robyn kass of kassting inc the first season is expected to premiere during the summer of 2020
