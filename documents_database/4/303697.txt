she is considered one of the most important luk thung vocalists in thailand the child of poor farmers pumpuang had only two years of primary education before her family s plight forced her to take to the fields as a sugar cane cutter although illiterate she was adept at memorising lyrics and participated in many local singing competitions at age 15 she came to the notice of a visiting band and from the late 1970s onwards her fame skyrocketed today she is remembered for her lyrics which told of thailand s rural poor she adapted pleng luk thung thai country music into a dance ready form known as electronic luk thung although loved by millions of fans her music career was marred by pumpuang s lovers managers and promoters who deprived her of her earnings to the extent that she could not afford treatment for a blood disorder that eventually caused her death at the age of 30 in 2018 she was featured as a google doodle on what would have been her 57th birthday
