in 1800 he married marianne wermerskirch they had five children and lived together in m nster he painted portraits of several noble and civic figures in north rhine westphalia including franz von f rstenberg count friedrich leopold zu stolberg stolberg gebhard leberecht von bl cher freiherr vom stein and princess pauline zur lippe as well as several families from m nster and petrus von hatzfeld the last abbot of marienfeld abbey he also painted portraits of working class subjects an innkeeper s daughter a locksmith s son a baker to the cathedral chapter a tutor and a singer in the prince bishop s chapel several of his paintings have been catalogued including those by him in the landesmuseum in m nster but others are lost or missing a large number of images have been cataloged but other images are missing
