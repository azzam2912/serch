he was born at water orton warwickshire and died at singleton lancashire watson was the fifth son of the vicar of water orton and was educated at st bees school and at pembroke college cambridge as a cricketer he was a right handed tail end batsman and a right arm fast medium bowler he had no success in any of his three games of first class cricket failing to take a single wicket although he matriculated in 1900 watson did not graduate with a bachelor of arts degree from cambridge university until 1910 he then took until 1921 to convert this to a master of arts he was ordained as a church of england priest and from 1912 to 1913 he served as the curate at all saints church in blackpool following service in the first world war in the royal army chaplains department he returned to the fylde area as the vicar of great singleton from 1919 and was there up to his death in 1944
