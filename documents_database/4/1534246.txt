after finishing primary school in 1933 he entered in the matei basarab high school where he was one of the top students by autumn 1939 georgescu took contact with communist ideals becoming a member of the clandestine union of communist youth utc in the following months he took part in several action of the group spreading manifestos and leaflets against carol ii s authoritarian regime and its alignment with hitler s germany he also organized a protest of the students with communist sympathies against an initiative of his high school that would have eliminated poor students soon after the utc nominated him as leader of a technical group responsible for propaganda work in bucharest s green sector in 1941 georgescu enrolled in the faculty of letters of the university of bucharest and in spring 1942 he was elected a member in the leadership of the university s utc cell there he continued the anti fascist propaganda by spreading manifestos condemning the pro fascist dictatorship of conduc tor ion antonescu and romania s collaboration with nazi germany in the invasion of the soviet union as well as presenting the achievements of the soviet army and of the other european resistance movements
