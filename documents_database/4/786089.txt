james bruce and mary linn bruce ca 1830 1907 he was educated at andes academy and graduated a b from rutgers college as valedictorian of the class of 1884 then he studied law in the office of cassius m shaw at andes n y and was admitted to the bar in 1889 in 1890 removed to new york city where he resided in the twenty first assembly district he was managing clerk in the office of hector m hitchings until 1892 and then opened his own law office in 1894 he married lillian ballantine knapp and they had four children in 1903 he was president of the new york county republican committee and conducted the unsuccessful campaign of mayor seth low for re election he was the lieutenant governor of new york from 1905 to 1906 elected in 1904 but defeated for re election in november 1906 although his running mate charles evans hughes was elected governor all other republican candidates were defeated by the nominees of the democratic independence league fusion ticket on december 5 1906 he resigned and was appointed by governor frank w higgins a justice of the new york supreme court to fill the vacancy caused
