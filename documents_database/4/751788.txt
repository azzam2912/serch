house representative from minnesota he was born at waterford mills indiana he attended the public schools and learned the printer s trade later he taught school and became superintendent of the millersburg indiana school district he left that vocation and was employed by the millersburg local newspaper in 1876 and afterward became editor and proprietor he moved to minnesota in 1882 and settled in glencoe in 1884 he moved to northfield and published the northfield news he was a delegate to the republican state conventions in 1886 and 1888 elected secretary of the republican state central committee in 1886 and 1888 and served as chairman in 1890 delegate to the republican national convention in 1888 he was appointed a member of the board of regents of the state university in 1890 he was president of the state editorial association he was an unsuccessful candidate for election in 1892 to the 53rd congress but was elected mayor of northfield in 1894 he was elected as a republican to the 54th 55th 56th and 57th congresses march 4 1895 march 3 1903 he served as chairman of the committee on ventilation and acoustics 55th congress he did not seek renomination in 1902
