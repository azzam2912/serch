in 2009 the national policy framework for technical and vocational education and training tvet was created to address the technological and human resource needs special emphasis was placed on providing equal opportunity for all guyanese to access technical education and training regardless of sex or gender in mid 2011 the guyanese education ministry completed the tvet enhancement project with financing from the caribbean development bank cdb gemsville technical university was established as a result of this initiative gemville technical university offers programs of industrial relevance in the areas of science engineering technology and management preparing students for leadership roles in the world of work gemsville technical university is registered with the national accreditation council nac under the ministry of education of the cooperative republic of guyana gemsville technical university is a member of the following international institutions
