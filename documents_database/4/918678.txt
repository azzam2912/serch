it comprises 63 flats taymount grange was built in 1935 to designs by george bertram carter on the site of taymount a 19th century house and the former queens tennis club the developer was sir malerham perks it has a steel frame with masonry infill and a rendered finish flat roof and metal streamlined windows the aesthetic is more style moderne being reliant in simplicity of detailing rather than ornamentation when built some of the flats comprised two apartments a large one for the main occupier and a smaller one for the servant the flats were advertised with the tag the servant problem solved there were also a restaurant lounge and guest rooms as well as seven tennis courts a swimming pool and a putting green michael gambon television film and theatre actor lived in the forest croft block for four years during his early to mid twenties
