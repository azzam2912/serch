in november 1994 she released her first single doy bi to pen to udedokei saturday pen and wristwatch in march 1995 ishimine won first prize at the 5th nhk s newcomer singing contest in may she released a cover version of the renowned song hana originally recorded by shoukichi kina for the film himeyuri no t the tower of himeyuri this her third single sold over 500 000 copies she was selected to participate in the 46th annual nhk k haku uta gassen with the song at the end of the year satoko ishimine was born the third girl of three sisters in naha city okinawa prefecture japan her family loved listening to such legendary foreign musicians as the carpenters and the beatles she started dreaming of becoming a professional singer when she was ten year old through the initiating years of her career ishimine established her popularity for the public as an okinawan singer especially due to the hit of her third single hana nevertheless she did not want to be regarded as such a matured vocalist with modest and magnificent songs as people liked her to perform those days after five years since the debut she started writing
