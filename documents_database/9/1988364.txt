in 669 severus consecrated bar easo as maphrian of the east during his tenure as patriarch severus pursued a policy of centralised authority within the church severus openly allied himself with caliph muawiyah i and utilised this alliance to forcibly impose his policies this led to a dispute over the rights of archbishops ecclesiastical jurisdiction and ordination rights eventually a schism developed between the patriarch and a faction led by sergius zkhunoyo and gabriel of rish ayno with the support of the monasteries of st matthew and of qartmin who disputed severus policies this faction was largely dominated by monks as opposed to laymen and clergymen the schism endured for the remainder of his tenure as patriarch and in 681 severus authorised john archbishop of the monastery of st matthew and bishops joseph and sergius to negotiate with the schismatics he died later that year
