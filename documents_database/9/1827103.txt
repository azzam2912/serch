it originally appeared in the ladies home journal in december 1926 it depicts two elderly musicians on flute and clarinet playing a duet while a young girl takes a break from sweeping to listen the painting s title appears on the sheet music from which the musicians play the love song depicts one of rockwell s common themes the contrast of youth and age through the wistful young girl and the elderly musicians although the main scene is linear and realistic like most of his work rockwell adds an impressionist landscape outside the window to demonstrate his range of talents rockwell s fascination with antique maps of which he had a sizable collection can be seen in the old map he uses to anchor the scene in rural america it is signed and dated in red in the lower right corner norman rockwell 26 rockwell was only thirty two when he was commissioned to paint the love song but he had already had a flourishing artistic career illustrating the saturday evening post for ten years by that point his fame grew rapidly in the years to come but he maintained a relationship with ladies home journal that lasted until near
