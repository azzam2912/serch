he has released eleven albums to date including one cafe su tan made up of his own versions of songs by jeremi przybora and jerzy wasowski and most have enjoyed considerable chart success his characteristic style consists of strong clear lyrics and music composed in special keys and harmonies using instruments such as piano played by himself saxophone violin and various horns influenced by such artists as marek grechuta and jan kanty pawlu kiewicz his music style has been described as soft jazz he was born on 31 july 1967 in krak w poland at age seventeen he won first prize grand prix at the student song festival in krak w in 1984 he went on to join the piwnica pod baranami cabaret composing such hits as zn w w drujemy and released his first album naprawd nie dzieje si nic really nothing is happening in 1991 he did participate in the aleksander glondys s ellington po krakowsku ellington krak w way a concert based upon idea of notable composers of piwnica pod baranami playing their interpretations of duke s music other participants include pawlu kiewicz zbigniew raj and several other musicians
