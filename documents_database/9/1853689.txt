reed born july 3 1990 is an american football tight end for the washington redskins of the national football league nfl he played college football at florida and was drafted by the redskins in the third round of the 2013 nfl draft a native of new britain connecticut attending new london high school as a junior he completed 122 of 222 passes for 1 706 yards and 38 touchdowns he threw only two interceptions and also rushed for 370 yards and eight touchdowns he led new london to an undefeated regular season in 2007 and was a finalist for the 2008 joe montana high school quarterback of the year considered a four star recruit by rivals com he was rated the 10th best dual threat quarterback in the nation reed accepted an athletic scholarship to attend the university of florida where he played for coach urban meyer and coach will muschamp s florida gators football teams from 2009 to 2012 originally recruited as a quarterback he did not appear in any games and was redshirted as a true freshman in 2009 after the 2009 season he shifted to the tight end position reed finished his college career with 78 receptions
