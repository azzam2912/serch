all 109 seats in the parliament were up for election the election was held simultaneously with the 2000 spanish general election incumbent socialist manuel chaves gonz lez won with a relative majority of 52 but was re elected president of the regional government of andalusia with the support of the andalusian party the parliament of andalusia was the devolved unicameral legislature of the autonomous community of andalusia having legislative power in regional matters as defined by the spanish constitution and the andalusian statute of autonomy as well as the ability to vote confidence in or withdraw it from a president of the regional government voting for the parliament was on the basis of universal suffrage which comprised all nationals over eighteen registered in andalusia and in full enjoyment of their political rights the 109 members of the parliament of andalusia were elected using the d hondt method and a closed list proportional representation with a threshold of 3 percent of valid votes which included blank ballots being applied in each constituency parties not reaching the threshold were not taken into consideration for seat distribution additionally the use of the d hondt method might result in an effective threshold over three
