the architectural design of the original yellow line station is by falc o e cunha on march 14 1977 the yellow line station was extended based on the architectural design of falc o e cunha and sanchez jorge on december 28 1996 the yellow line station s northern atrium was refurbished and on may 17 1997 the yellow line station s southern atrium was also refurbished both based on the architectural design by paulo brito da silva on august 29 2009 the red line station was built based on the architectural design by germano venade and located under avenida duque d vila serving the avenidas novas area of the city and the nearby instituto superior t cnico on the same day the yellow line station s northern atrium was again refurbished based on the architectural design of paulo brito da silva and sofia carrilho
