at the beginning of the 15th century the church was turned over to the knights of the order of the spade ordine della spada subsequently circa 1575 the church and the order of the spada was forbidden to carry out their investitures by the viceroy igo l pez de hurtado de mendoza and the ceremonies were moved to the san giacomo degli spagnoli in 1775 the church was granted to the complementari who restored the church in a baroque style the present church of san giacomo was reconstructed by reopening an alternative older portal surmounted by a coat of arms of a shell with crossed swords due to the damage from the bombardment of 1943 and the irpinia earthquake of 1980 the building is not longer consecrated and used for temporary art exhibitions the interior artwork has been moved elsewhere
