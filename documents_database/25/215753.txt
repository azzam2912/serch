laney born january 19 1949 in dallas texas is an attorney an administrator of transportation programs and a republican supporter and fundraiser laney grew up in dallas texas where he graduated from st mark s school of texas in 1967 he then graduated from stanford university and the law school at southern methodist university laney practiced at the law firm of jenkens gilchrist from 1977 to 2003 and was managing partner of that firm from 1990 to 2002 between 2003 and 2007 he was a partner with the law firm of jackson walker llp in dallas where he practiced in the area of corporate and financial law laney was chair of amtrak s board of directors from 2003 to 2007 he was previously the chairman of the texas transportation commission laney became a campaign pioneer for george w bush by raising over 100 000 for his 2000 presidential campaign he also made significant donations to republican campaigns in the 2008 elections texas governor william clements appointed laney to the texas finance commission from 1989 to 1995 that committee was responsible for regulatory oversight of state chartered banking and thrift institutions he served as governor bush s texas commissioner of transportation
