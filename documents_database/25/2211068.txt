a former samling and jette parker young artist gringyt trained as a pianist before commencing her studies at the lithuanian academy of music and theatre before joining the royal welsh college of music and london s national opera studio during her time as a member of the jette parker young artists programme gringyt s roles included maddalena rigoletto flora bervoix la traviata wood nymph rusalka maddalena il viaggio a reims innocent the minotaur albina la donna del lago and suzy la rondine gringyt made her house debut in the 2013 14 season as maddalena in guisseppe verdi s rigoletto before appearing with the welsh national opera as fenena in nabucco in 2014 15 gringyt reprised the role of maddalena at the royal opera house and the bolshoi and debuted the role of h nsel in h nsel und gretel for the vilnius city opera the 2014 15 season also saw gringyt debut the title role in carmen a role in which she has received critical acclaim her debut recording the complete rachmaninov songs with pianist iain burnside was nominated for a 2014 gramophone award in 2015 gringyt won in the category of young singer at the international opera awards
