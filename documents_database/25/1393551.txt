francis russell 7th duke of bedford lord george russell and john russell 1st earl russell were his elder half brothers and lord edward russell and lord alexander russell his full brothers an amateur cricketer russell played first class cricket for the marylebone cricket club between 1833 and 1846 russell was a lieutenant colonel in both the 52nd regiment and the royal horse guards in 1832 he was returned to parliament for bedfordshire a seat he held until 1841 and again briefly in 1847 in 1848 he was appointed serjeant at arms of the house of commons which he remained until 1875 russell married isabella clarissa daughter of william griffith davies in 1834 they had two sons of whom the youngest was george w e russell and three daughters lady charles russell died in june 1884 russell survived her by ten years and died in june 1894 aged 87
