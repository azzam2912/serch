is a 501 c 3 non profit organization located in tarzana california launched by aaron dunn in 2005 it aims to set music free by providing music to the public free of charge without copyright restrictions musopen provides an online library of public domain music recordings and sheet music it also raises money to finance classical music recordings which are released into the public domain in 2008 musopen released newly commissioned recordings of the 32 beethoven piano sonatas into the public domain in 2010 the site organized a fundraiser via kickstarter to commission recordings of a larger repertoire raising a total of 68 359 more than six times their initial target of 11 000 in july 2012 musopen announced that the editing of the recordings was finished after which the audio files were uploaded to its website and archive org the final list of music was announced in august 2012 in september 2013 a new kickstarter fundraiser was launched by musopen to record the complete works of fr d ric chopin the fundraiser was successful exceeding the funding goal of 75 000 by over 15 000 all recording for this project has been finished and editing was completed in april
