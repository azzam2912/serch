together with the county of sovana it was one of the two subdivisions into which the possessions of the aldobrandeschi then lords of much of southern tuscany were split in 1274 at the moments of its creation it included part of today s province of grosseto up to the isola del giglio and castiglione d orcia in what is now the province of siena in the 14th century the republic of siena was able to capture isola del giglio roccastrada istia d ombrone magliano in toscana selvena arcidosso and castiglione d orcia reducing the county to its capital castell azzara semproniano and scansano in 1439 after the marriage of bosio i sforza and the last aldobrandeschi heir cecilia the county was inherited by the sforza family who would become ruler of the duchy of milan and owned also other possessions in tuscany and the marche the sovereignty of the county was ceded to the grand duchy of tuscany in 1633
