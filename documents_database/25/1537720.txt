despite having had no prior acting experience castro quickly became involved in work on the esu stage after getting cast in several mainstage productions with both the theater and music departments at esu he became a part of hollow tree films a local film production company he played the lead role of malcolm in the 2009 hollow tree films production of the road home which was nominated for best feature film and best screenplay at the 2009 pocono mountains film festival upon achieving early success in theater and film he officially left football at esu after his freshman year to pursue an acting career in december 2012 he made his tv debut in the gossip girl episode the revengers as a police detective castro currently resides in new york city
