cornell s governorship in albany under the provisions of the new york constitution of 1846 32 senators and 128 assemblymen were elected in single seat districts senators for a two year term assemblymen for a one year term the senatorial districts were made up of entire counties except new york county seven districts and kings county three districts the assembly districts were made up of entire towns or city wards forming a contiguous area all within the same county at this time there were two major political parties the republican party and the democratic party the republicans were split into two factions the stalwarts and the half breeds the greenback party also nominated a ticket the new york state election 1880 was held on november 2 the only statewide elective office up for election was carried by a republican the approximate party strength at this election as expressed by the vote for chief judge of the court of appeals was republican 563 000 democratic 518 000 and greenback 13 000 the legislature met for the regular session at the state capitol in albany on january 4 1881 and adjourned on july 23 george h sharpe r was re elected speaker
