he is a member of the communist party of india marxist kerala state committee dinesan was born on 31 december 1969 in memunda vatakara as youngest son of th kunjiraman nambair and deviamma his father th kunjiraman nambiar was a famous folklore artist who won kerala sangeetha nataka academy award for his contribution towards folklore dinesan did his early education from memunda higher secondary school vatakara he did his post graduation in malayalam ma malayalam from the university of calicut campus he also holds a b ed and completed his ll b from kerala law academy thiruvananthapuram puthalath dinesan entered politics through the students federation of india he went on to become the state president of the students federation for two years in 2000 and 2001 he later became all india vice president of the students federation he also was general secretary of calicut university union he was editor of student monthly of sfi student he is faculty in e m s academy he is also editor of marxist samvadam he became a communist party of india marxist state committee member in 2015 he became political secretary to the chief minister of kerala when pinarayi vijayan assumed office in 2016
