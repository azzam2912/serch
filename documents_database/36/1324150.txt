john 1822 1859 was a british travel writer and biographer one of the four sons of james augustus st john who went on to become journalists and authors of some literary distinction bayle st john began contributing to periodicals when only thirteen and when twenty he wrote a series of papers for fraser under the title de re vehiculari or a comic history of chariots to the same magazine he contributed a series of essays on montaigne and in 1857 he published montaigne the essayist a biography in four volumes during a residence of two years in egypt he wrote the libyan desert 1849 and while in egypt he learnt arabic and visited the oasis of siwa on his return he settled for some time in paris and published two years in a levantine family 1850 and views in the oasis of siwah 1850 after a second visit to the east he published village life in egypt 1852 purple tints of paris characters and manners in the new empire 1854 the louvre or biography of a museum 1855 the subalpine kingdom or experiences and studies in savoy 1856 travels of an arab merchant in the soudan 1854 maretimo a story
