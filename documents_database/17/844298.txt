the story was initially serialised in spirou magazine before released as a hardcover album in 1985 in the comet s watchmaker the count de champignac goes to visit his great nephew and entrusts the castle to spirou and fantasio who both hope to relax however a strange vessel crashes in their garden and an individual resembling the count de champignac accompanied by a strange creature snouffelaire disembark the individual proves to be the descendant of the count aur lien come from the future to look for the seeds of some plants that are extinct in his time in order to preserve them located in the forest of palombia knowing about the hostile environment of the palombian jungle spirou proposes that he and fantasio accompany aur lien however a malfunction sends the vessel to the sixteenth century during the portuguese colonization of palombie spirou fantasio and aur lien are confronted by the hostile cannibals and the colonists who take them for french spies finally aur lien s vessel disintegrates but strange individuals come from even further in the future to save them and to bring them back to their respective times
