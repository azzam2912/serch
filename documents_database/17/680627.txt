the film held the top spot at the box office in 1967 manoj kumar started his mr bharat persona and his brand of patriotism and fearless courage with this film manoj kumar played a village man bharat who sacrifices everything to get his brother educated his brother puran prem chopra goes abroad and comes back a selfish man wanting his share of the property but when india starts a war with pakistan bharat decides to enlist and head to the border while his selfish brother with the help of his greedy uncle charandas madan puri and some partners try to gain profit by selling drugs and black marketeering in the market in the end bharat returns as a demoralized soldier and his brother repents as he is caught by the police by the brother in law of bharat hence he vows to be a good and hardworking brother just like his indo pak war veteran brother bharat the movie also stars asha parekh as a doctor promoting family planning kamini kaushal and pran in his first positive character role madan puri played the main villain with ease full ability and command the film won several major awards many years later
