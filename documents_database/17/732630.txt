in 2013 the parish merged into the new parish abrantes s o vicente e s o jo o e alferrarede the population in 2011 was 1 699 in an area of 2 21 km it was one of the three parishes that make up the urbanized area of the city the origin of s o jo o has its base in the early settlement of abrantes first documented in 1176 afonso henriques granted its castle to the order of santiago de la espada in 1173 three years later he granted its fish channel to the monastery of lorv o the grant was partly in aid of the residents but entitled the king to nine tenths of the catch in 1179 afonso henriques granted a foral to abrantes which was confirmed by king afonso ii in 1217 on 24 april 1281 king denis granted abrantes to his wife queen elizabeth of portugal he continued to expand the fort building the detention block with the aim of expanding the settlement he extended its jurisdiction to include punhete today s const ncia in the 17th and 18th centuries abrantes was an important military base classified a pra a de guerra de 1 ordem
