it is sited above the town of hausach in the county of ortenaukreis in the german state of baden w rttemberg the original hill castle recorded for the first time in 1246 as castrum husen was probably built here by the dukes of z hringen in the 12th century it later went into the possession of the counts of freiburg in 1303 the castle went to the counts of f rstenberg as a result of the marriage of verena of freiburg count henry iv of f rstenberg had the castle extensively remodelled and expanded from 1453 to 1477 a neck ditch and an outer ward guarded the heart of the castle with its palas and bergfried the castle was the residence of the prince s castellans burgv gte in 1632 it burned down and was rebuilt in 1643 french weimar troops under jean baptiste budes de gu briant destroyed the castle later the ruins were incorporated into fortified earthworks or schanzen in order to defend the valley against enemy troops in 1896 the bergfried was given battlements instead of the tower s conical roof in 1968 the town of hausach purchased the ruins from the house of f rstenberg today
