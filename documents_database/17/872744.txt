it is added as one of two appendices including the equally eschatological rule of the congregation following the book of the community rule on one of the first seven scrolls discovered at the qumran site the rule of the blessing includes three benedictions for use during the eschaton one for the general assembly of the eschatological tribe of israel which describes a sort of living water bringing them into a new covenant with god one concerning the kohen priest sons of zadok chosen by god who will act like angels and lead israel after the war the third prayer is that for the messianic meal to bless the prince or davidic messiah who has come to deliver israel similar prayers are found elsewhere in the scrolls and some believe that this particular manuscript may be a collection of prayers for general daily use
