after various attempts to create a memorial as an homage to the participants of the uprising the inauguration of the monument took place on may 2 1840 the anniversary of the event on november 22 1985 king juan carlos i re inaugurated the monument as a memorial to all those who gave their life for spain including those that died in conflicts other than the peninsular war since then a flame fuelled by gas has been constantly burning on the front of the monument this parallels other war memorials around the world of national symbolic importance frequently known as the tomb of the unknown soldier
