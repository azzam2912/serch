rajendra babu and produced by vizag raju the film features prabhakar vinaya prasad and srinath in the lead roles the film was widely popular for the songs composed and written by hamsalekha upon release the film is a remake of malayalam hit akashadoothu 1993 directed by sibi malayil and was subsequently remade in telugu as mathrudevobhava 1993 both the versions had actress madhavi in the lead role however in kannada version she was replaced by vinaya prasad this film won best film filmfare award a church like hindu order provides the spiritual context for this unusual story about the extinction of a lower class alcoholic family the drunkard mohan tiger prabhakar is married to sharada vinaya prasad a cultured music teacher employed in a hindu ashram who tries to reform her husband the villain in this allegory of a hindu community is a christian antony who tries to rape sharada and later kills mohan shortly after sharada is diagnosed as terminally ill with cancer sharada dies before she can find adoptive parents for her children but the saintly head of the ashram srinath does this for her the film also has a few comic scenes e g in a bar
