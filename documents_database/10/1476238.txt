the wrestler jim dryden 1907 1974 was his father he received his education at king s college at the 1962 british empire and commonwealth games he won the silver medal as part of the men s eight alongside crew members leslie arthur darien boswell colin cordes alan grey christian larsen louis lobel robert page and alan webster after having received an invitation to the henley royal regatta he won the inaugural prince philip challenge cup regatta in 1963 in henley on thames that year the henley regatta was regarded as the event that came closest to a world championship darien boswell peter masfen and dudley storey made up the other rowers and bob page was the cox the same coxed four team then went to the 1964 summer olympics in tokyo where they placed a disappointing eighth at the 1968 summer olympics in mexico he was part of the men s eight that came fourth in the final dryden was later the president of the auckland rowing club dryden s son murdoch dryden would later represent new zealand at world rowing championships
