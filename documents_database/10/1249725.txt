his career spanned more than forty years during which he acted in a number of media both within pakistan and abroad including the european film traffik he mostly acted in sindhi films and dramas in the 1960s and early 1970s after the downfall of the sindhi film industry anokha moved to lahore and worked on films television shows and theater in the early 1980s he returned to karachi and worked in television and on the stage until the end of his life he remained associated with the entertainment industry he earned many awards in his career including the nigar awards and the ntm viewers choice awards for best actor for the drama serial kashkol and also received an award from the culture department of the government of sindh for his performance in the theater drama azadi kay mujrim malik anokha died on 26 july 2008 due to a heart attack he was buried at the sakhi hassan graveyard in karachi he was survived by his widow three daughters and a son
