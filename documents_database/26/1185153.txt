the most common german word for lollipop is lutscher but like its english counterpart sucker it has come to be supplemented in modern times by lollipop the band debuted in july 2000 with a self titled album which contains what are today many of their most famous songs since then they have released new cds videos and audiobooks at the rate of about two per year as of 2005 their label had sold more than 1 5 million of their cds and videos of which 8 made it to gold and one platinum note that the meanings of the terms platinum and gold are different for different countries in germany gold signifies 100 000 sales and platinum 200 000 the cast of the lollipops changes every few years in order that they maintain girls between the ages of about 11 and 16 as singers most of the time there are two lead singers with occasional accompaniments by other singers who are not named for a short time there were three main singers the current duet started recording in late 2007 with susi and fiona then 12 and 11 years old respectively and released an album called einfach tierisch in march
