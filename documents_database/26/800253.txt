as blue team captain he won 8 bermuda bowls 1 world team olympiad and 3 european bridge league s championships in the period from 1951 to 1966 perroux was born in mirandola modena in 1905 the son of alberto perroux and enrica maramotti perroux a criminal attorney in private life he was the captain of the team through 1950s and early 1960s he was the main craftsman of the team s cohesion and spirit both by supporting and consoling and by exhorting and forcing he was known for imposing tough discipline players who broke the rules were unconditionally benched regardless of short term benefits he even checked whether the team members went to bed alone and on time during tournaments perroux was the president of italian bridge federation from 1952 to 1967 when he retired to brazil and began helping brazilian players he wrote one book the blue team our story of bridge whose revised and enlarged edition was published in 1973
