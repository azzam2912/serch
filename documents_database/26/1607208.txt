it was orchestrated by jessica wells it is the first opera written by an indigenous australian and involving an indigenous cast it is based on the february 1939 cummeragunja walk off in which cheetham s grandparents were involved pecan summer was commissioned for the olympic arts festival held in association with the 2012 summer olympics in london the libretto was written by deborah cheetham during a short stay in lucca italy the opera had its official world premiere at the westside performing arts centre mooroopna victoria on 8 october 2010 where it was presented by the short black opera company and the melbourne chamber orchestra under david kram it had an unofficial preview performance in melbourne in july 2010 with his agreement cheetham used a recording of then prime minister kevin rudd s february 2008 parliamentary apology to the stolen generations as part of the work the timeline of the opera moves from the dreamtime to july 2006 on the banks of the yarra river near federation square in melbourne to 1939 on the banks of the dhungala murray river near the cummeragunja mission to several months later in winter 1939 to shepparton at an unspecified time to federation square
