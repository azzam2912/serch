kumanovo is also the name of the city where the municipal seat is found kumanovo municipality is part of the northeastern statistical region the municipality has an area of 509 48 km and borders lipkovo municipality ilinden municipality and ara inovo municipality to the west serbia to the north sveti nikole municipality and petrovec municipality to the south and staro nagori ane municipality and kratovo municipality to the east kumanovo as an inhabited area date from 1519 the most detailed data comes from evliya elebi who traveled to the region the area was inhabited by 52 families and 300 residents after the karposh uprising in 1689 kumanovo entered a period of growth stagnation after 1945 kumanovo experienced fast economic administrative and cultural development by the 2003 territorial division of the republic ora ac municipality was merged with kumanovo as was part of the rural kle evce municipality the other part of kle evce was attached to staro nagori ane municipality kumanovo had 94 589 inhabitants in 1994 and 103 205 at the last national census the present day municipality has 105 484 inhabitants which makes it the most populous municipality in macedonia according to the 2002 census ethnic groups in
