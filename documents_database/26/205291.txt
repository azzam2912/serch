a sequel the widows of eastwick was published in 2008 the story set in the fictional rhode island town of eastwick in the late 1960s follows the witches alexandra spofford jane smart and sukie rougemont who acquired their powers after leaving or being left by their husbands although alexandra is a widow their coven is upset by the arrival of darryl van horne who buys a neglected mansion outside of town the mysterious darryl seduces each of the women encouraging their creative powers and creating a scandal in the town the power of the three witches grows so much so that they unknowingly bewitch the townsfolk they come in contact with this becomes clear when sukie s lover and boss clyde gabriel kills his busybody wife felicia before hanging himself the three women share darryl in relative peace until he unexpectedly marries their young innocent friend jenny the gabriels daughter the witches resolve to take revenge by giving her cancer through their magic although alexandra feels remorse for their hex the spell kills jenny and darryl flees town with her younger brother chris apparently his lover in his wake he leaves their relationships strained and their sense of self in
