the object number of cultural heritage of regional significance is 661720983680005 the gostiny dvor was built in the period from 1820 1840s and was reconstructed at the end of the 19th century it was included in the general plan of the town in 1848 it has a rectangular form building with an open center structurally the building is divided into two parts the northern part which overlooking the cathedral square and the southern part overlooking lenin street former bolshaya moskovskaya street the gostiny dvor forms the southeast corner of the cathedral square it refers to the historical shopping facilities of the urals at the end of the 19th century the main entrance of the building is inside the courtyard the entire construction is focused on a voluminous composition the partitions divide the inner space into the rows of the cell rooms the original interior decor was lost the authors of the project are not known
