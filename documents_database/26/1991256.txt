it is based on the 1965 selma to montgomery voting rights marches led by james bevel hosea williams martin luther king jr and john lewis the film stars actors david oyelowo as king tom wilkinson as president lyndon b johnson tim roth as george wallace carmen ejogo as coretta scott king and common as bevel selma premiered at the american film institute festival on november 11 2014 began a limited us release on december 25 and expanded into wide theatrical release on january 9 2015 two months before the 50th anniversary of the march the film was re released on march 20 2015 in honor of the 50th anniversary of the historical march selma had four golden globe award nominations including best motion picture drama best director and best actor and won for best original song it was also nominated for best picture and won best original song at the 87th academy awards the film received a generally positive critical reception but was met with criticism for its historical innacuracies largely centered on the perceived villification of johnson and the omission of several prominent jewish civil rights leaders in 1964 dr martin luther king jr of the southern christian leadership
