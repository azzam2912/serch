also if an act of valour was deemed insufficient for the medal of military valour the war merit cross could be awarded instead from 1922 onwards a bronze sword on the ribbon showed this class of award the war merit cross was in bronze 38mm wide 1 1 2 inches with a blue ribbon a second award was indicated by a bronze star on the ribbon the reverse side bears a 5 pointed star on a background of rays the obverse has the royal cypher ve iii under a crown in the upper arm merito di gverra war merit on the horizontal arms and a roman sword point upwards on oak leaves in the lower arm during world war ii the war merit cross underwent a number of significant changes in 1941 by decree the war merit cross was replaced by the medal of military valour
