in editorial bruguera she published a couple of stories sissi 1957 and cuentos de andersen 1958 which are numbers 38 and 57 of the series stories later she worked in editorial susaeta using her reputation to present collections such as las mu ecas pascual maria and mu ecas recortables de mar a pascual however her most important work was done with grupo oc ano cuentos infantils la biblia infantil f bulas las mil y una noches aprenda ingl s con mar a pascual aprendo matem ticas mi primer diccionario el sexo contado a los peque os etc among the most important though they were not very well known as they were intended for south america and several european countries her personal works include more than 2 000 drawings and are preserved in the biblioteca de catalunya
