loud has performed as a soloist with most of the major uk orchestras and also performs chamber music in 2001 she created her one woman show which features a wide range of repertoire including classical jazz and film music in 2003 nicola loud was awarded an aram by the royal academy of music in london and in 2008 became a television presenter for the bbc co hosting the concerto final of bbc young musician of the year in cardiff as a past winner she was also featured in a documentary celebrating 30 years of the competition and provided the commentary for the eurovision young musicians 2008 program on bbc four in 2012 nicola married rupert allason who writes about espionage under the pen name nigel west
