he was recruited by the metro goldwyn mayer studios while a law student in paris at the age of 25 bergerac met and married ginger rogers with whom he appeared in twist of fate 1954 also known as beautiful stranger he then appeared as armand duval in a television production of camille for kraft television theatre opposite signe hasso he played the comte de provence in jean delannoy s film marie antoinette queen of france in strange intruder 1956 he shared the screen with edmund purdom and ida lupino and in les girls 1957 he played the second male lead he also appeared in gigi 1958 thunder in the sun 1959 the cult horror film the hypnotic eye 1960 and a global affair 1964 in 1957 he received the golden globe award for foreign newcomer he appeared in a few more films and on television including batman 77 sunset strip alfred hitchcock presents 3 episodes the lucy show get smart the dick van dyke show and perry mason season 7 episode 19 his last appearance was on an episode of the doris day show in 1969 after which he left show business and became the head of revlon s paris
