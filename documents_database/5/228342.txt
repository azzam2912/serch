in 1983 he briefly moved to england featuring in a number of productions there in 1985 he branched into film acting with a minor role in a telemovie version of oscar wilde s lady windermere s fan over the next few years he moved between film and theatre starring in productions such as macbeth and king lear as well as several supporting roles in films telemovies mini series and regular series these included notable australian films blackrock and oscar and lucinda he has had guest roles in the series the secret life of us stingers and farscape morrell starred as harry in australia s first production of michael gow s play away which was performed by the griffin theatre company and premiered at the stables theatre sydney on 7 january 1986 after filming oscar and lucinda morrell won the role of forensic scientist lance fisk in the series murder call which screened from 1997 to 1999 in 2000 he won one of his most significant roles to date as the protagonist of the abc series grass roots the series was not renewed after the 2001 season and morrell followed this with a major role in the abc telemovie marking time
