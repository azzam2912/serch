in 1969 he was awarded for the honored artist of the byelorussian ssr and in 1975 luzanin was awarded with the honorary badge meritorious activist of culture of people s republic of poland he was born on 20 october 1909 in the village prusy modern day salihorsk district minsk region in belarus his notable poems including kroki shari 1928 and adnaha osna 1931 u anin s poem were written in russian and polish language in 1928 he studied at minsk belarusian pedagogial college in 1933 he met his wife ja hienija pflaumbaum in siberia he studied at the literary linguistic department and graduated at belarusian state university he participated in the literary association ma adniak and uzvy a he worked at the magazine company uzvy a magazine in 1930 until 1931 and from 1931 until 1933 he was a broadcaster at the belarusian radio u anin was arrested by ogpu soviet secret police in 1933 charged with membership in a counter revolutionary organization and sentenced to 2 years of exile to mariinsk now in kemerovo oblast he was released a few months later he worked as an editor of the main edition of the literature on mechanical engineering in moscow
