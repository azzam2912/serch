the film was originally a senior thesis project also involved were olivia vaughn and christine meyer originally a 12 minute short film the film was expanded into a feature length documentary when the crew was awarded a 10 000 dollar grant from the sprout fund in 2013 in 2014 christine meyer and olivia vaughn became interns at the animal media group a production and visual effects studio previously animal had produced the documentary blood brother which won two awards at the 2013 sundance film festival animal allowed the crew to use their facilities to edit the footage of fursonas later animal offered to be the official production company for the documentary the documentary premiered at the 2016 slamdance film festival fursonas was well received winning a spirit of slamdance award at its debut film festival both variety and deadline hollywood featured positive reviews of fursonas on their websites
