amboy bank has locations in middlesex and monmouth counties in central new jersey and serves consumer and commercial customers its deposits are insured to the legal maximum by the federal deposit insurance corporation fdic the bank was chartered on may 12 1888 as the first national bank of south amboy by a group of prominent local businessmen in the late 1950s the bank began to open branches in the local area and in 1960 the name was changed to first national bank of south amboy madison township to reflect the move of its headquarters to nearby madison township which was later renamed old bridge township in 1963 the name was changed to amboy madison national bank and in 1991 it was shortened to amboy national bank the bank is now known as amboy bank a name change that occurred in 2008 after the bank converted to a state charter amboy bank has been privately owned since its inception and has been held by amboy bancorporation since 1984 amboy direct was established in 1995 to provide innovative products to those outside of amboy bank s traditional footprint in 2003 it transitioned to offering accounts that could be opened online in 1998
