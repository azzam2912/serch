by 1910 the jewish community in the neighbourhood of byculla had increased to the extent that the synagogue could no longer service all the devotees and the synagogue was extended with the help of jacob david sassoon s grandson the synagogue is one of the largest in asia outside of israel within the extensive grounds of the synagogue there are two jewish schools which are operated by the sir jacob sassoon high school trust and the e e e sassoon high school trust in which jewish children were originally educated over time most of the bagdadi jews moved to the more affluent colaba area or abroad to israel australia britain and canada with the scarcity of jewish students the schools have opened to all communities and currently provide for the population in the vicinity which is 98 muslim in 2011 for the celebration of the 150th anniversary the synagogue was restored
