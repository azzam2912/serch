community leaders and the long time merchants succeeded in keeping the market open beginning in december 2008 the market was managed by the brooklyn economic development corporation until nyc edc was reassigned site management of the market in december 2014 merchants operating at the market are mainly from spanish speaking caribbean countries or latin america and primarily sell products such as fruit vegetables crafts herbs candles and ethnic food from their respective countries the market also serves as an economic and cultural anchor for williamsburg and its community regularly holding events for the public the moore street market merchants association represents the fifteen vendor s of the market the market is located on 110 moore street in brooklyn and is accessible via the new york city subway s at the flushing avenue station
