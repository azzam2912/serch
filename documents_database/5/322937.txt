in his early years correia was journalist at the newspaper rep blica as a member of the portuguese government headed by prime minister ant nio guterres in the early 2000s correia played a major part in the establishment of the loja do cidad o the citizen s shop a one stop shop intended to improve quality in public services and aimed to develop a new philosophy of more efficient and modern public service throughout the major cities of portugal he was elected as a candidate of the socialist party to the european parliament in the european parliament elections he was a former chairman of the associa o acad mica de coimbra o a f in 1995 the major football club of coimbra and rdp the state managed public radio network of portugal he died of a heart attack on 9 october 2007 in brussels he had been living and working in belgium as a member of the european parliament since 2004
