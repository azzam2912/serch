porfilio received a bachelor of arts degree from the university of denver in 1956 and a bachelor of laws from the university of denver college of law in 1959 he was in private practice in denver from 1959 to 1962 he was an assistant state attorney general of colorado from 1962 to 1968 he was a deputy state attorney general of colorado from 1968 to 1972 he was the state attorney general of colorado from 1972 to 1975 porfilio served as a united states bankruptcy judge of the district of colorado from 1975 to 1982 on may 18 1982 porfilio was nominated by president ronald reagan to a seat on the united states district court for the district of colorado vacated by judge fred m winner porfilio was confirmed by the united states senate on june 24 1982 and received his commission on june 25 1982 porfilio served in that capacity until may 13 1985 due to elevation to the tenth circuit on april 5 1985 reagan nominated porfilio to a seat on the united states court of appeals for the tenth circuit vacated by judge robert hugh mcwilliams jr porfilio was confirmed by the senate on may 3 1985
