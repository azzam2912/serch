the two platforms are as built and are only 5 cars in length the station s ceiling was originally fitted with glass in order to let natural light in it has green faience plaques and mosaic name tablets by heins lafarge grueby faience company from 1904 the ceiling was also decorated with ornamental motifs the station is no longer accessible from the street but its graffiti covered walls can be seen through the windows of any lexington avenue local train as well as from express trains if no local train blocks the view
