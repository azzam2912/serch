peixoto was the first brazilian woman to receive a doctorate in mathematics and the first brazilian woman to join the brazilian academy of sciences peixoto graduated from the federal university of rio de janeiro in 1943 with a degree in engineering having also studied mathematics at the university and acted as a monitor for the university s national faculty of philosophy in 1948 she received a doctorate in mathematics and began teaching at the escola polit cnica da ufrj in 1949 peixoto published on the inequalities formula 1 in following her work on convex functions peixoto was appointed an associate member of the brazilian academy of science on 12 june 1951 she was the first brazilian woman to join the organization and the second woman after marie curie a foreign associate of the academy peixoto married maur cio peixoto in 1946 the pair jointly published structural stability in the plane with enlarged boundary conditions in 1959 one of several papers which led to peixoto s theorem peixoto had two children marta and ricardo with maur cio peixoto
