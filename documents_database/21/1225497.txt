executive power is exercised by the regional government legislative power is vested in both the government and the regional council of sardinia the regional council of sardinia consiglio regionale della sardegna is composed of 60 members the assembly is elected for a five year term but if the president suffers a vote of no confidence resigns or dies under the simul stabunt vel simul cadent clause also the assembly will be dissolved and there will be a fresh election the regional government giunta regionale is presided by the president of the region presidente della regione who is elected for a five year term and is composed by the president and the ministers assessori who are currently 12 sardinia is also divided in 377 comuni municipalities which have even more history having been established in the middle ages when they were the main places of government the latest regional election took place on 24 february 2019 christian solinas of the sardinian action party was elected president
