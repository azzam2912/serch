the team plays their home games at h e b center at cedar park the team has made the postseason in 8 out of their 14 seasons in the nba development league on october 15 2014 after the san antonio spurs purchased the franchise the team colors and logo were changed to reflect the silver and black motif used by the spurs the austin spurs were established in columbus georgia as the columbus riverdragons the franchise in 2005 was sold to southwest basketball llc and were relocated to the city of austin texas following the relocation the franchise changed their name and logo becoming the austin toros which was unveiled on august 10 2005 the toros name was the only nba associated team and first d league team to possess a nickname of spanish origin the toros began play during the 2005 06 season on june 28 2007 the toros were acquired by the san antonio spurs becoming the second d league team to be owned by an nba team after the los angeles d fenders were purchased by the los angeles lakers in 2006 on august 9 2010 the toros announced they would move to the cedar park center
