the turning point was the construction of the swedish railway system the southern main line railway finished in 1864 passed through n ssj later other railways were inaugurated whereby n ssj due to its geographical location in the country became an important junction a line to katrineholm was opened in 1874 and that year also the opening of the oskarshamn line the one to halmstad in 1882 to kalmar in 1914 today n ssj is the only swedish junction where railways in six different directions meet as a result industries moved to n ssj and the population increased the most important industries were wood industries due to the forest covered sm land province area at a time when it had become possible to factory manufacture the wooden chair a k a windsor chair n ssj attempted to get a charter which was granted in 1914 making it one of the now defunct cities of sweden it is since 1971 instead the seat of the much larger n ssj municipality its population continued to increase throughout the 20th century in the 1940s production of electrical installation equipment began and the industry eldon ab was for a long time the largest industry
