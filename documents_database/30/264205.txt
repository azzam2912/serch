jamal al durrah and his 12 year old son muhammad were filmed by talal abu rahma a palestinian cameraman freelancing for france 2 as they were caught in crossfire between israeli and palestinian security forces the footage shows the pair crouching behind a concrete cylinder the boy crying and the father waving then a burst of gunfire and dust after which the boy is seen slumped across his father s legs fifty nine seconds of the footage were broadcast in france with a voiceover from charles enderlin the station s bureau chief in israel who was not present during the shooting based on information from the cameraman enderlin told viewers that the al durrahs had been the target of fire from the israeli positions and that the boy had died after an emotional public funeral muhammad was hailed throughout the muslim world as a martyr over the months and years that followed commentators questioned the accuracy of france 2 s report the israel defense forces accepted responsibility for the shooting at first but later retracted french journalists who saw the raw footage confirmed that france 2 had cut a final few seconds in which muhammad appeared to lift his hand
