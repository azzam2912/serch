the theoretical basis of keturi v jai initially was futurism which arrived through russia from the west and it later was influenced by cubism dadaism surrealism unanimism and german expressionism the most influential futurist for lithuanian writers was russian poet vladimir mayakovsky members of keturi v jai included founder kazys binkis antanas rimydis b 1905 juozas tysliava b 1902 salys emerys b 1898 juozas eng b 1899 and teofilis tilvytis b 1903 poet adomas jak tas was a fierce critic of the keturi v jai movement he objected to its literary principles aesthetics and philosophy the only poet of that generation not attracted to keturi v jai was stasys santvaras who was more interested in symbolism and expressionism than in modern forms of poetry the poetry of dadaist pranas mork nas 1900 1941 has been characterized as a continuation of rebellion started by keturi v jai against traditional poetry the lithuanian school keturi v jai in dublin ireland is named after the literary movement
