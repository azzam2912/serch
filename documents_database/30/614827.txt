his education included a b s from western college in shenandoah iowa in 1889 and art classes from the art students league of new york he worked as an artist and reporter for the san francisco examiner an editorial and feature writer for the san francisco chronicle and a war correspondent for the evening bulletin in japan and china he wrote short stories and screen plays including for two black and white silent movies the story for a blowout at santa banana 1914 and the scenario for the movie the fuel of life 1917 he also lectured on the occult in this period he was also a promoter of the international language esperanto and he was the editor of the san francisco esperantist after world war i wallace built a small cabin in the forest near carmel california which he used as a laboratory for experimenting with telepathy which he sometimes referred to as mental radio he made hundreds of drawings charts diagrams and writings attempting to reveal the patterns of life including reincarnation communication with intelligent life on other planets and with dead spirits he wrote about messages from the dead from ancient greeks ancient egyptians vikings and atlanteans
