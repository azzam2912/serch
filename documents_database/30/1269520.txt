mishnah brurah 206 12 although the taz adds the word u mori and my master which would lengthen the permitted window of opportunity prior to consuming any food or beverage a jew must recite a blessing to express his or her gratitude to god for providing sustenance b berachot 35a and there are different blessings for different types of food if for instance an individual was just about to take a bite from a carrot for which the blessing ends borei pri ha adamah the creator of fruits of ground but instead erroneously concluded the blessing that applies to fruit borei pri ha eitz the creator of fruits of the tree correcting the suffix of the blessing to the appropriate conclusion toch k dei dibur i e within the allotted 3 second window of time the error would thus be resolved
