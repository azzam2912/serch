his mother took out a lease on the shirburn estate from her brother in 1505 which passed to edward on her death in 1525 he served as high sheriff of berkshire and oxfordshire for 1505 06 and 1517 18 he was made keeper of woodstock park in 1508 and an esquire of the body in 1509 in 1512 he led 30 men in sir william sandys company in an unsuccessful expedition to biscay led by thomas grey 2nd marquess of dorset to assist king ferdinand s invasion of france he then commanded a ship during naval hostilities early in 1513 joined the royal army in northern france in the summer and was knighted at tournai later that year he served at a royal banquet at greenwich in july 1517 and in 1520 attended both henry viii s meeting with the king of france at the field of cloth of gold and his subsequent meeting with the charles v holy roman emperor at gravelines he represented wallingford in parliament in 1529 he died in 1543 he had married by 1504 cecily the daughter of sir john verney of pendley hertfordshire with whom he had at least 3 sons sir leonard chamberlain
