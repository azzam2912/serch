he started working at the end of the fifties and was pretty active as a composer of film scores signing about 120 sound tracks especially for spaghetti westerns and commedie sexy all italiana films his piece one silver dollar the main theme to giorgio ferroni s blood for a silver dollar 1965 was later included in quentin tarantino s inglourious basterds he was also well known for his work in pop music particularly for his collaboration with mina for whom he composed among others the hit song parole parole and wrote arrangements and orchestrations for numerous of her songs and albums the last collaboration with mina was for her 2012 album 12 american song book for which ferrio traditionally provided the string arrangements he was the official conductor for sanremo music festival in 1959 and 1962 and for the eurovision song contest 1965 he also took part as conductor in several important italian tv shows ferrio was married to ballerina and film actress alba arnova
