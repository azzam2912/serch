as with many of her class the ship was never named instead she was referred to by her hull designation the ship was laid down on 30 september 1942 under maritime commission marcom contract mc hull 981 by kaiser shipyards vancouver washington launched 3 november 1942 sponsored by mrs eugene e blazier and commissioned on 18 february 1943 lieutenant eskil a bjork usnr in command during world war ii lst 461 was assigned to the asiatic pacific theater she took part in the capture and occupation of saipan in june and july 1944 in the capture and occupation of tinian in july 1944 the leyte operation in october 1944 the lingayen gulf landings in january 1945 in the nasugbu operations in january 1945 and the assault and occupation of okinawa gunto in may 1945 following the war lst 461 returned to the united states and was decommissioned on 2 september 1947 and struck from the navy list on 16 september that same year on 30 march 1948 the tank landing ship was sold to consolidated builders inc of seattle washington and subsequently scrapped lst 461 earned six battle stars for her world war ii service online resources
