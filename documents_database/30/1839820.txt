the lyrics are about pippi longstocking s great grandfather fabian fabbe longstocking who was a feared pirate captain on all seven seas the last line of the chorus tjo hej hadelittan lej is a nonsense phrase the song was originally performed by pippi longstocking actress inger nilsson who published it in 1975 as b side of the single h r kommer pippi l ngstrump in 2001 it was covered as a crossover version with the melody of dio s holy diver by the swedish metal band black ingvars other swedish versions are by plura jonsson in 1999 by siw and tove malmkvist in 2003 and by johanna gr ssner and mika pohjola who performed a jazz version of this song in 2006 a norwegian version with lyrics by marit trulsrud is by anita hegerland and frank cook from 1970 there is also a german version called seer uber opa fabian with lyrics by helmut harun it was first performed by actress eva mattes in 1970 and covered in 2012 by german singer lena meyer landrut for the compilation album giraffenaffen
