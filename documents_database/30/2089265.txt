the film is directed by anjan dutt this is the fourth installment of byomkesh series by anjan dutt the film is based on kohen kobi kalidas by sharadindu bandyopadhyay unlike the previous three installments jisshu sengupta portrayed the character bakshi in this one replacing abir chatterjee the curtain rises on a small coalfield town plagued with a series of unexplained troubles that broke out suddenly owner of a coalmine seeks the help of byomkesh bakshi to sort things out for him when byomkesh arrives with his trusted confidante ajit they discover to their complete dismay a maze of crimes woven around a violent murder that had little to do with the job that they were entrusted with a mesmerizing mosaic of criminal characters a sordid saga of lecherous lust the thriller unfolds the complex layers of the human mind so powerfully penned by sharadindu now retold by the master movie maker anjan dutt byomkesh eventually cracks the case but seals it with his own sense of justice upon release the movie received moderately positive reviews from the critics while it was well received by the public a hint for a sequel next case chiriyakhana was given at the final scene
