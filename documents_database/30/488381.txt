in 2008 he was given the kyung ahm prize his studio is in gyeongju south korea yoon specializes in his own variants on the traditional korean ceramic style called buncheong he has modified this style using angular shapes decorated with brushwork in white he often represents aspects of kyongju in his work yoon kwang cho was born in hamhung present day north korea he attended school in south korea graduating from hong ik university in seoul in 1973 he then studied at the karatsu kiln in japan an age old center for korean ceramics becoming inspired to work in the traditional korean pottery known as buncheong
