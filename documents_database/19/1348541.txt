the u s navy acquired her on 21 january 1918 from her owner george a cormack of new york city for use during world war i placing her in service as uss resolute designated either sp 3003 or id 3003 the difference in reported designation occurring perhaps because the u s navy sometimes used the sp and id no designations interchangeably for civilian vessels procured for world war i service resolute served her entire u s navy career as a ship s tender for the transport uss mount vernon id 4508 and was carried aboard mount vernon for use at mount vernon s various ports of call records of resolute s career otherwise are lacking mount vernon was decommissioned in september 1919 and it is likely that resolute was disposed of perhaps by sale around that time
