his father charles murray was superintendent of education in the colony and his great grandfather was the well known christian pastor andrew murray he read classics at victoria college today the university of stellenbosch and then studied at worcester college oxford he returned to south africa in 1910 and soon thereafter was called to the bar in pretoria he was appointed queen s counsel in 1932 and five years became a judge on the transvaal bench in 1955 he accepted an offer from the neighbouring territory of southern rhodesia to become its chief justice he took office on 1 august that year he was knighted on 14 august 1958 on 9 march 1961 his 73rd birthday he stepped down and was replaced by sir hugh beadle murray retired to plettenberg bay cape province where he died at the age of 88 on 10 may 1976
