freegle ltd is a non profit organization an industrial and provident society for community benefit registration no 32410r and registered as a charity with hmrc reference xt32865 in september 2018 there were 426 groups supported by about 350 volunteers with 2 753 649 members in the uk the majority of freegle groups are hosted on freegle direct freegle s own open source platform norfolk groups have their own platform a lot freegle groups are also accessible via the trash nothing website in april 2015 the freegle mobile app was launched to allow access to freegle direct groups on android ios and kindle phones and tablets
