the rachis is green with the upper part clearly curved the linear leaflets 10 12 cm long are arranged on the rachis in the opposite way with an obtuse angle of insertion which varies from 45 to 80 the margins are integer and smooth the leaves of the basal part are often reduced to spines it is a dioecious species with ovoid shaped male cones bluish green in color 18 25 cm long and 5 7 5 cm wide the female cones about the same shape are 20 30 cm long and have a diameter of 10 15 cm both are produced in numbers ranging from one to three the seeds 20 30 mm long have an oblong shape and are covered by a red or yellow sarcotesta
