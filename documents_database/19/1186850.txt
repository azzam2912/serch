he started playing ba lama at the age of six and dropped out of high school to pursue a career in music he completed his first album g n olayd if it had been day when he was only sixteen years old in 1989 he demonstrated his mastery of the technique of ba lama on his second album gece ile g nd z aras nda between night and day his third album r zgar n kanatlar the wings of the wind was released in 1991 later that year he married yeter g ltekin on 2 july 1993 he went to sivas to take part in the pir sultan abdal cultural festival alongside many other musicians authors poets and intellectuals he died at the age of 22 during the arson attack on the madimak hotel where he was staying his wife gave birth to their son roni hasret g ltekin three months after his death
