when detective sergeant ryder s wife is kidnapped along with nuclear scientists from the california power station where they all work he sets out to find her facing resistance from within his own police department he leaves his job and begins taking the law into his own hands his son jeff a highway patrolman and a few other trusted friends attempt to stop the terrorists from detonating home built atomic bombs along california s fault lines which would unleash massive earthquakes killing millions of people and destroying california s major cities the inspiration for the plot appears to be acknowledged by maclean himself in his preface to the 1977 edition of the book where he describes his first experience of an earthquake while in california on 9 february 1971 maclean said in a 1975 interview he was working on a book where half california fell into the ocean it s called for 1982 when all the planets are in alignment he said there are people who believe it the more i get into it the more i believe it myself the book became a best seller
