he paints in a style he describes as sacrocubism which portrays sacred events with several features of the post cubist art movement cocco sant ngelo was born in concepci n del uruguay entre r os argentina an argentine city that lies on the western shores of the uruguay river raised catholic he married myriam verbauwen in february 1962 they joined the church of jesus christ of latter day saints in june of that year becoming argentine pioneers due to their financial situation at the time and the lack of temples in south america sant ngelo and his wife were not able to be sealed together in the temple until 11 years after their baptisms they traveled to utah and were sealed in the salt lake city temple on october 4 1973 four years later they were sealed as a family to five of their children in the bern switzerland temple cocco sant ngelo likes to experiment with his art and explore new styles and cultures eventually settling on sacrocubism as he experimented with approaches and mediums he made a living teaching art and art history moving for his career the family traveled to ibiza spain puebla mexico back to argentina
