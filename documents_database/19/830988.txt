born march 29 1927 is a businessman and a republican politician from charleston south carolina the charleston born ravenel served in the united states marine corps from 1945 to 1946 he thereafter received a bachelor of science degree from the college of charleston in 1950 he is a realtor and general contractor he was a democratic member of the south carolina house of representatives from 1953 to 1959 he became a republican in the early 1960s and ran many times for office he lost elections for the south carolina state senate five times 1962 1974 and 1976 for the united states house of representatives 1971 special election and for mayor of charleston also 1971 ravenel was elected as a republican to the south carolina senate in 1980 he served until 1986 when he was elected to the u s congress from the charleston based 1st district he was reelected three more times without serious opposition he did not run for reelection in 1994 but instead ran for governor he finished second in the republican primary to then state representative david beasley but lost the runoff beasley considered more conservative than ravenel went on to win the general election in 1996
