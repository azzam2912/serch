the river descends over its course in its upper reaches the feeder creeks of the wingecarribee form the wingecarribee swamp the only substantial peat bog in new south wales most of the swamp has been drained and the remaining section of the swamp is the habitat of a number of endangered species the river runs through a valley on the plateau that is home to a popular camp biloela the moss vale road crosses the river at bong bong between bowral and moss vale at berrima the river is crossed by the hume freeway the shoalhaven scheme pumps water from the shoalhaven river into the wingecarribee reservoir and this water supply augments the water supply for greater metropolitan sydney
