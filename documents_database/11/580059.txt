he founded and occupied the second chair of the brazilian academy of letters from 1897 until his death in 1934 he was also the president of the aforementioned academy in 1926 coelho neto was born in the city of caxias maranh o on february 21 1864 his father was portuguese and his mother was an indigenous woman ana silvestre coelho at six years of age his parents moved to rio de janeiro he began his education at the externato of the colegio pedro ii he attempted medical school but soon gave up in 1883 he enrolled at the university of s o paulo school of law living in the boarding house where also lived raul pompeia who attended the academy of s o paulo at that time he soon found himself involved in a student movement against a professor in anticipation of reprisals he moved to the law faculty of recife where he completed the first year of law having been a student of the jurist and poet tobias barreto returning to s o paulo he devoted himself passionately to the abolitionist and republican campaign an attitude that led to new frictions with the university of s o paulo school
