she is the granddaughter of dorival caymmi daughter of danilo and simone caymmi and niece of nana and dori caymmi before opting for a musical life alice studied law afterwards she studied performing arts c nicas at pontif cia universidade cat lica do rio de janeiro her first professional recording was at the age of 12 in her aunt nana s album desejo more specifically at the track seus olhos written by her sister juliana shortly after they performed the song with nana at a sold out canec o in 2007 she took part of the ending ceremony of the 2007 pan american games with her father danilo in 2012 via a partnership between kuarup and sony music she released her first self titled album followed by a number of tours the album is mainly original except for two cover songs one originally by dorival caymmi and another by bj rk two years later she released her second album rainha dos raios this time with mostly cover versions and just two new songs including a partnership with michael sullivan in 2015 her version of como v s was featured at rede globo s miniseries felizes para sempre in october 2015
