the extremely low budget film was directed by michael rae and produced by charles band who is widely known for producing b movies starring kim milford cheryl smith and gianni russo the film features keenan wynn and roddy mcdowall and marked the screen debut of actor eddie deezen the reptilian alien creatures in the film were works of stop motion animation by animator david w allen marking the first chapter in a decades long history of collaboration between allen and band the alien spacecraft model featured in laserblast was designed and built by greg jein in two weeks and the musical score was written in five days by joel goldsmith and richard band the first film score for both composers laserblast has received overwhelmingly negative reviews and consistently ranks among the bottom 100 list of films on the internet movie database many critical reviews however cited allen s stop motion animation as one of the film s only redeeming qualities a sequel was planned for 1988 but ultimately abandoned due to financial difficulties laserblast was featured in the seventh season finale of the comedy television series mystery science theater 3000 marking the show s final episode on comedy central before
