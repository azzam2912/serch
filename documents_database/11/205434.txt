china zorrilla she was a disciple of margarita xirgu and moved to argentina in 1971 where she had a distinguished career in theater tv and movies also in spain and other southamerican countries her big successes in movies besos en la frente esperando la carroza waiting for the hearse elsa fred and conversations with mother for which she won the moscow film festival award for best actress his granddaughter guma zorrilla was a theater costume designer his house in montevideo is now a museum he is featured on the 20 peso note
