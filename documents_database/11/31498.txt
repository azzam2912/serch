nanna is a sumerian deity the son of enlil and ninlil and became identified with the semitic s n the two chief seats of nanna s s n s worship were ur in the south of mesopotamia and harran in the north a moon god by the same name was also worshipped in south arabia the original meaning of the name nanna is unknown the earliest spelling found in ur and uruk is lak 32 na where na is to be understood as a phonetic complement the name of ur spelled cuneiform lak 32 unug urim is itself derived from the theonym and means the abode unug of nanna lak 32 he was also the father of ishkur the pre classical sign lak 32 later collapses with e the ideogram for brother and the classical sumerian spelling is e ki with the phonetic reading na an na the technical term for the crescent moon could also refer to the deity cuneiform u sakar later the name is spelled logographically as nanna the semitic moon god su en sin is in origin a separate deity from sumerian nanna but from the akkadian empire period the two undergo syncretization and are identified
