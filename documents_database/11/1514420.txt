jackson became the ensemble s sole director in 1988 during the 1980s and 90s the group gave almost 90 concerts in france and spain including performing and recording early music connected to montr al and new france in 2015 andrew mcanerney was appointed artistic director following the death of christopher jackson the studio produces an annual season of ten concerts in montr al and appears regularly at festivals throughout canada and internationally notable collaborations have included jordi savall emma kirkby ton koopman skip semp guillemette laurens matthias maute noel edison suzie leblanc and daniel taylor countertenor the ensemble is flexible drawing on a regular core of professional vocalists and instrumentalists season concerts vary from small a capella with 12 14 singers through to large scale combined orchestra and chorus with a chamber orchestra made up of period instruments since its inception the group has released 17 recordings most recently on the atma classique label
