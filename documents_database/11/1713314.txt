gelispie is the instructor of jazz drums at the michigan state university college of music he has been a professional jazz drummer since he started playing with a 10 piece band in the ninth grade gelispie has traveled on the road with wes montgomery sonny stitt dinah washington etta jones lou donaldson and gene ammons in the detroit area he has performed with joe williams nancy wilson dizzy gillespie tommy flanagan barry harris oliver jones o c smith al hibbler and most recently with geri allen blues musicians he has worked with include jimmy witherspoon john lee hooker and big maybelle locally he has played with many great jazz musicians including donald walden marcus belgrave perry hughes rodney whitaker gary schunk rick roe marion hayden bill heid andrew speight and wycliffe gordon with sonny stitt with gene ludwig with donald walden with bill heid
