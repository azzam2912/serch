1510 was an italian painter and woodcarver active in genoa born in alessandria he was released from his familial bonds patria potesta in 1453 when he is documented to have established a workshop in genoa giovanni s father giacomo was also a painter and the family is first mentioned in 1414 in regards to a rental dispute his first documented piece is from 1463 for a relief carving of a chapel s altar in genoa cathedral at which time he would have met vincenzo foppa who came to liguria as specified by that contract and who would later influence mazone s style he received another carving contract in 1476 to renovate the main altar in the cathedral his workshop specialized in making wood frames for polyptychs and documentation shows he ran a busy establishment in the mid 1460s he made a polyptych now dispersed of saint nicholas heavily decorated with gilded relief work his annunciation polyptych now held at santa maria di castello shows netherlandish influences particularly in the rendering of the central panel jan van eyck s portable altarpiece the dresden triptych was in liguria in italy by the end of the 15th century and apparently influenced mazone s
