currently socialist youth front often cooperates with the red green alliance and around 80 percent of suf members are also members of the red green alliance though suf itself retains an extraparliamentary focus socialist youth front is a uniting organisation for the left wing radical youth and it consists of many ideological persuasions ranging from leninists democratic socialists to anarchists the politics and doctrine of suf is however based on an anti parliamentarian revolutionary and marxist world view the organisation publishes the external magazine avanti as well as the internal newsletter blomster og barrikader flowers and barricades the highest authority of the socialist youth front is the national congress which is held twice a year once a year 9 members are elected to the board of leadership which consists of 25 members the last 16 being regional representatives who are elected locally
