william richardson linton 2 april 1850 in diddington huntingdonshire 7 april 1908 in ashbourne derbyshire corpus christi college m a was an english botanist and vicar of the parish of shirley derbyshire he was regarded as one of the leading batologists of his day linton was born in diddington in huntingdonshire in 1850 he married alice shirley daughter of rev walter waddington shirley and philippa frances emilia knight shirley on 26 january 1887 with whom he had one daughter viola marion linton he became the vicar of st michael s church in shirley linton collected botanical specimens and records often working with his elder brother who was also a cleric rev edward francis linton was based mainly in edmondsham in dorset in 1890 w r linton published a short article in the journal of botany describing a new species of hawkweed hieracium holophyllum found in derbyshire in 1892 he and his brother published a short eight page guide called some scottish willows which they followed two years later with set of british willows linton wrote an extensive book about the flora of derbyshire published in 1903 the front cover of his flora contained a large illustration in gold leaf of
