in 1934 he received a phd from the university of munich emminger was a lecturer at his alma mater he began to work at the deutsche bundesbank in october 1950 from 1 april 1953 to 31 december 1969 he was board member of the bank in addition he served as west germany s executive director at the international monetary fund from 1953 to 1959 he was vice president of the bank from 1 january 1970 to 31 may 1977 he was appointed president of the bank on 1 june 1977 succeeding karl klasen in the post karl otto p hl became his deputy emminger s term lasted until 31 december 1979 he was replaced by p hl in the post in july 1986 he began to serve as an advisor to the philippines government corazon aquino had become president in february 1986 on behalf of west germany at the same time he published his memoirs emminger died of a heart attack in manila the philippines at the age of 74 in august 1986
