frank born 1956 is the vice chairman of oaktree capital management having previously served as managing principal john bernkopf frank was born in 1956 the son of sally n e bernkopf and howard a frank his father was a professor of surgery at the harvard medical school his mother was a financial administrator at the children s hospital in boston he attended wesleyan university where he graduated with a b a in history with honors afterwards he became a legislative assistant to robert f drinan a member of congress frank later received his j d from university of michigan law school graduating magna cum laude in law school he was managing editor of the michigan law review and a member of the order of the coif he is a member of the state bar of california after graduating from the university of michigan law school frank became a law clerk for frank m coffin of the united states court of appeals for the first circuit in 1984 he went to munger tolles olson while in private practice he was listed in woodward and white s best lawyers in america in july 2001 he joined oaktree capital management as general counsel
