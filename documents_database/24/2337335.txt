k paramasivan is an indian politician who served as member of lok sabha the lower house of the parliament of india between 1962 and 1967 he is commonly known as skp or skp thatha thatha is grandfather in tamil skp was born in sinniampalayam a village near erode in then madras presidency as the tenth child to kumarasamy gounder and kuppayi ammal skp joined indian national congress he was the president of muthugoundenpalayam panchayat for 10 years before becoming the member of parliament from erode constituency in 1962 he has held office as the president of coimbatore district congress committee he has also held several positions like the head of sinniyampalayam milk producers cooperative bank for 35 years and erode district milk producers cooperative union for 4 years when acharya vinoba bhave started bhoodhan movement many landlords donated part of their land to poor and the landless in tamilnadu skp donated a part of very fertile land he owned to landless farmers he also made generous donations during the india china war lal bahadur shastri started the national dairy development board nddb in 1964 dr kurien chose skp to lead the effort in tamilnadu through skp s efforts aavin dairy
