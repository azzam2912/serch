in 1924 along with two other notable amatieris footballers ark dijs pavlovs and arnolds tauri brams joined rfk as it went on to its first latvian league title brams went on to winning two more league titles with rfk in 1925 and 1926 at the end of the 1927 season brams was one of several rfk main squad footballers together with arv ds jurgens voldem rs plade eslavs stan iks and others to leave the team for the newly founded riga vanderer club which became a strong rival for rfk in the following years rfk was usually the stronger side on field but there was always a high tension surrounding the derby between these two sides in 1928 rv with brams as one its key players won the riga class b tournament to progress to class a which it won the following year in 1930 brams returned together with vanderer to the top tier of latvian football winning the bronze medals of virsl ga he retired from top level football in 1931 in later years brams played in football tournaments for clerks as a member of the squad of the latvian customs office brams played 8 international matches for latvia
