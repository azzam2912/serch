as the king s daughter she was a fille de france and was known at court by the traditional honorific of madame royale because she was the king s eldest surviving daughter she did not survive childhood dying at the age of five due to tuberculosis marie th r se was born 2 january 1667 at the ch teau de saint germain en laye her parents had her baptised in the palais du louvre in 1668 her parents reputedly adored the young girl and her mother wanted her to become the queen of her native spain as a fille de france marie th r se was entitled by law to the style of her royal highness but was referred to simply as madame royale she was also known as la petite madame to distinguish her from her aunts the wives of her uncle monsieur who were known as the first madame henrietta of england died 1670 and the second madame elisabeth charlotte of the palatinate died 1722 the young marie th r se died on 1 march 1672 at the ch teau de saint germain en laye and was buried at the royal basilica of saint denis outside paris france
