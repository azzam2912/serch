his career is closely linked to that of the emperor julian he was master of the cavalry and in 362 served as consul nevitta is first mentioned regarding a battle against the alamanni who had been pillaging the region of raetia in 357 the romans were led by barbatio and ammianus marcellinus writes that nevitta commander of a troop of cavalry and afterwards consul was present and conducted himself manfully in 361 julian promoted nevitta to the rank of magister equitum when julian decided to move against constantius in a bid for power nevitta was one of his two generals the other being jovinus the bulk of julian s force was divided between these two it was nevitta s task to lead his army through raetia and then guard the pass of succi thus protecting julian s rear as the caesar moved on to constantinople ammianus took the opportunity to describe nevitta at this point in his career a man neither in high birth experience nor renown comparable with those on whom constantius conferred the highest magistracy but on the contrary somewhat boorish and what was more intolerable cruel in his high office when the emperor julian set up a
