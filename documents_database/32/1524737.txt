the blunt smooth protoconch contains about one whorl the teleoconch contains the five subsequent whorls the suture is appressed and obscure the spiral sculpture is apparently absent the axial sculpture shows on the body whorl six strong stout wavelike ribs continuous up the spire with wide interspaces and practically vertical0 the surface appears to be smooth but as the specimen is slightly beach worn some minute sculpture may have disappeared the suture is undulated by the ribs there is no anal fasciole perceptible the anal sulcus is narrow short with a subsutural callus in front of it the outer lip is thin sharp with a marked varicose swelling behind it there are no internal lirae the inner lip is callous the smooth columella is very short stout and straight the siphonal canal is hardly differentiated from the aperture but deeply cut this species occurs in the pacific ocean off panama
