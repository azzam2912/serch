the film is based on bo yang s novel the alien realm which is based on the true story of the republic of china army s 93rd division taking refuge in santikhiri mae salong on the border between myanmar and thailand in the early 1950s the chinese civil war has ended with the defeat of republic of china and the establishment of the people s republic of china by the communists soldiers from the 93rd division of the republic of china army take their families with them and leave southwestern china by entering northern burma myanmar the hike through the jungle is full of disasters and shortages of supplies but the survivors reach and settle within the china burma border they build a village and form an alliance with a local armed gang to resist attacks from the burmese government later the relocated government of the republic of china offers to take the soldiers and their families to taiwan but some are disappointed with the government and decide to stay
