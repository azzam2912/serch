james jones born february 4 1943 is a canadian politician he served in the house of commons of canada from 1997 to 2000 initially as a progressive conservative and later as a member of the canadian alliance he was later succeeded by former minister of national defence john mccallum jones was born in warwick ontario and received a degree in business administration from ryerson polytechnical institute in 1967 he became a certified general accountant in 1971 jones was an internal auditor of the moore corporation from 1967 to 1969 and a marketing manager at ibm canada ltd from 1969 to 1997 in 2007 jones plead guilty to assaulting a maid at a hotel in niagara on the lake citing a momentary lapse in judgement he had originally been charged with sexual assault following the incident he received a conditional discharge and was sentenced to 12 months probation and 20 hours of community service he began his political career at the municipal level serving as a councillor in markham s third ward from 1988 to 1997 he was elected to the canadian house of commons in the 1997 federal election defeating liberal candidate gobinder randhawa by 3 639 votes in markham
