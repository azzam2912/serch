an open buy back is a money market instrument used to raise short term capital it is a form of borrowing using nigerian government securities as collateral it is an open ended transaction with both parties maintaining the right of liquidation or a roll over without prior notice within trading hours of the day an obb transaction is usually sealed between dealers of different banks and settlement is made at central bank where bills are transferred from the borrower s bills holding to the lenders bills portfolio on the other hand funds equivalent to the face value of the treasury bills is moved from the account of the lender to the borrower s current account with the central bank of nigeria this product offers flexibility but with interest rate and bank stability risk it is a product for short term liquidity obb offers the benefits of treasury bills counts as liquid asset while removing the rediscounting risk associated with carrying treasury bills a casual look at obb might liken it to a repo though obb and repo reverse repo involve the exchange of cash for security with an agreement to buy back a repo has a predetermined repurchase date while
