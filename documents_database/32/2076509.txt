sheffer ii born march 25 1948 is an american lawyer university professor and politician from new york he was born on march 25 1948 he attended school in williamsville erie county new york he graduated b a from wheaton college in wheaton illinois he graduated j d from syracuse university college of law he entered politics as a republican and was mayor of williamsville from 1977 to 1978 he was a member of the new york state assembly from 1979 to 1988 sitting in the 183rd 184th 185th 186th and 187th new york state legislatures he was a member of the new york state senate from 1989 to 1993 sitting in the 188th 189th and 190th new york state legislatures he resigned his seat on september 19 1993 to pursue an academic career at the university at buffalo beginning in january 1994 he taught at the university at buffalo law school and the school of architecture and planning he also held administrative posts at the university and was director of the institute for local governance and regional growth from 1997 to 2005
