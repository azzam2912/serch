hughes hubbard or hhr is a new york city based international law firm among those that the american lawyer calls the top firms among the nation s legal elite the firm s history dates back to the late 19th century when it counted among its partners former u s supreme court chief justice charles evans hughes hughes hubbard s work has been recognized by several leading publications including chambers partners the legal 500 global arbitration review global restructuring review airfinance journal and latinfinance magazine for the twelfth time the american lawyer named the firm to its a list of the country s 20 leading law firms hughes hubbard has also been one of the country s leaders in promoting diversity in law firms in 2017 the firm received the gold standard certification from the women in law empowerment forum wilef for the seventh consecutive year one of only 15 firms nationally to achieve recertification seven times in a row the firm also received a perfect score of 100 percent on the 2016 2017 and 2018 corporate equality index a national benchmarking survey and report on corporate policies and practices related to lgbt workplace equality administered by the human rights campaign
