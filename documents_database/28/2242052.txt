this book contains the prophecies attributed to the prophet isaiah and is one of the books of the prophets chapters 40 55 are known as deutero isaiah and date from the time of the israelites exile in babylon according to john skinner this chapter which is largely a recapitulation of certain outstanding themes of the prophecy consists of exhortations addressed to the exiles in the near prospect of deliverance the original text was written in hebrew language this chapter is divided into 22 verses some early manuscripts containing the text of this chapter in hebrew are of the masoretic text tradition which includes the codex cairensis 895 the petersburg codex of the prophets 916 aleppo codex 10th century codex leningradensis 1008 fragments containing parts of this chapter were found among the dead sea scrolls 3rd century bc or later there is also a translation into koine greek known as the septuagint made in the last few centuries bc extant ancient manuscripts of the septuagint version include codex vaticanus b formula 1 4th century codex sinaiticus s bhk formula 1 4th century codex alexandrinus a formula 1 5th century and codex marchalianus q formula 1 6th century the parashah sections listed
