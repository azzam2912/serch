in 1974 kip founded rosie s place in boston america s first shelter for homeless women she founded this shelter in an abandoned supermarket after discovering that homeless women disguised themselves as men in hopes of getting into male only shelters kip was also one of the founders of victory house in the south end of boston a residential alcoholism treatment program for homeless alcoholic men in 1980 kip and fran froehlich co founded the poor people s united fund and from 1988 to 1990 they were fellows at the bunting institute at radcliffe college kip s longtime companion edith nicholson died in the 1990s and she married donna pomponio in 2004 kip herself died july 2 2011 of cancer
