during the 1970s huis ter duin was bought by the noorlander family the hotel has always focused on affluent tourists thus the royal families of belgium and the netherlands stayed there regularly other guests included princess grace of monaco writer thomas mann and film actors liz taylor and richard burton during the second world war the hotel was requisitioned by the germans for the post of ss officers that data was later used in the film soldaat van oranje which has been partially around the hotel a fire destroyed a large historic part of the hotel at january 25 1990 then huis ter duin was rebuilt and expanded in its current style many internationally known guests stayed in this hotel
