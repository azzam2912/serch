all male residents of cyprus who are of military age 16 and over are required to obtain an exit visa from the ministry of defense the republic of cyprus has an active military draft conscripts enlisting as of the 2016 winter draft have to serve a term of 14 months and military service continues as a reservist after the end of the term reservists are called up several times a year for 1 or 2 days at a time each year until the age of 50 at which point they can choose to voluntarily continue their military service if conscientious objector status is claimed due to religious or ideological reasons the objector may serve either an alternative military or civil service depending on the circumstances of each case enlisting had been optional for members of the armenian maronite and roman catholic minorities but as of 2008 service was made compulsory for them as well reduction of military conscription in cyprus
