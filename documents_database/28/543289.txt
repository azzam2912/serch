the mountains are located northeast of the old woman mountains southwest of the piute range and east of the fenner valley which includes the small communities of essex goffs and fenner the mojave national preserve is to the northwest and the bigelow cholla garden wilderness and sacramento mountains are to the east the piute mountains exhibit strong color contrast and texture that vary from very angular jagged volcanics to rounded smooth granite hills and the ridges are cut by numerous canyons and washes the piute mountains wilderness area is in the piute mountains and the surrounding bajadas and extensive alluvial fans below us route 66 the wilderness is within mojave trails national monument and managed by the bureau of land management blm dominant vegetation is creosote bush scrub which gradually changes into a mixed desert scrub at higher elevations the large bajadas provide excellent habitat for the threatened desert tortoise the entire wilderness area has been identified as critical habitat for the desert tortoise
