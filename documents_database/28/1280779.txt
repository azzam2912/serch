sen senkov has been published in numerous literary journals in russia and abroad he has published seven books of poetry prose poems and visual poetry recent publications include slash moscow 2008 in collaboration with alexei tsvetkov jr published at dmitry kuzmin s argo risk press and a god suffering from astrophilia new literary observer press moscow 2008 sen senkov s work has been translated into english italian serbian montenegrin german french estonian albanian dutch ukrainian slovenian and polish a collection of his work in english translation anatomical theater translated by ainsley morse and peter golub is published in 2013 with zephyr press he was awarded the turgenev festival prize for short prose in 1998 and in 2006 was nominated for andrei bely prize sen senkov work takes many forms he writes poetry short prose cycles and visual poetry and has collaborated with sound and video artists he has been quoted saying that the poem lives inside of me small naked formless you always write about one and the same thing just with different words
