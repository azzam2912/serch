he represented toronto in the legislative assembly of ontario from 1886 to 1894 and west toronto from 1896 to 1904 and toronto centre from 1904 to 1905 in the house of commons of canada as a conservative member he was also a member of the orange order in canada he was born in bailieboro county cavan ireland in 1850 the son of merchant richard clarke and came to toronto in 1864 after the death of his father he apprenticed as a printer with the toronto globe later working with the toronto mail in 1872 he was one of the leaders of a printers strike in the city clarke was the editor and publisher of the sentinel a weekly newspaper associated with the orange order which was widely distributed throughout north america between 1877 and 1896 in 1884 he married charlotte elizabeth scott he also served as mayor of toronto and was the manager of the excelsior life insurance company of toronto he died at home in toronto in 1905
