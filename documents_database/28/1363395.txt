dumont s love story is not to be confused with another dramatic anthology with the same name which ran on nbc in the 1970s or a game show also titled love story which aired on cbs from 1955 1956 love story aired live on tuesday nights at 9 pm est on most dumont affiliates the series was produced by david lowe this series was hampered by a small budget and starred mostly lesser known actors latter day critics such as castleman and podrazik 1982 have cited love story among other dumont series as one of the reasons fewer and fewer viewers tuned in to the ailing dumont network they called the series a simpering romance anthology that like several other dumont programs during the 1953 1954 season was doomed from the start by third rate scripts and cheap production the series did not last long and the network itself began crumbling shortly thereafter
