the work was completed between 1999 and 2005 the materials in the pieces range from sealskin to african mud cloth from embroidered silk to gossamer wings of butterflies the inaugural exhibition of the completed quilt of belonging and the launch of the quilt of belonging book took place april 1 2005 at the canadian museum of civilization in gatineau ottawa the quilt which was designed as a traveling exhibit then went on a five year tour across canada including communities in the arctic it was the art exhibit of its kind to tour in inuit communities in 2017 as part of canada s 150th birthday celebration the quilt of belonging is making another tour across canada including a three week stop at the canadian national exhibition in august
