mise en abyme is the french term for a similar literary device also referring to the practice in heraldry of placing the image of a small shield on a larger shield a story within a story can be used in all types of narration novels short stories plays television programs films poems songs and philosophical essays the inner stories are told either simply to entertain or more usually to act as an example to the other characters in either case the story often has a symbolic and psychological significance for the characters in the outer story there is often some parallel between the two stories and the fiction of the inner story is used to reveal the truth in the outer story often the stories within a story are used to satirize views not only in the outer story but also in the real world when a story is told within another instead of being told as part of the plot it allows the author to play on the reader s perceptions of the characters the motives and the reliability of the storyteller are automatically in question stories within a story may disclose the background of characters or events tell
