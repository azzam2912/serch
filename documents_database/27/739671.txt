from the writer director team of roger evan larry and sandra tomc a romantic thriller crossing follows the story of macho danarama sebastian spence who makes a deathbed promise to his father to turn their family from petty gangsters to upright brokerage owners the only thing standing in daniel s way is his newly discovered desire to dress up like a girl embroiling himself in an intense romance with a blackmailing prostitute crystal buble daniel must find his way through a maze of disgrace and pleasure to discover where his true loyalties lie crossing played in canadian theatres in 2007 and was nominated for 6 leo awards including best picture best directing and best screenplay it sold in over twenty countries roger larry is a director writer producer of film tv and new media who also makes visual art his latest cool daddy is a feature documentary currently in post production cool daddy is funded by the canadian media fund and canada s doc channel his most recent completed feature is the documentary citizen marc it was critically lauded and had the largest theatrical release for a canadian feature doc in 2014 roger was a creative producer on contemporary artist
