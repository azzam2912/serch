it was maintained by john mashey and various others dick haight alan glasser the pwb shell was released with version 5 unix or in mid 1975 sources differ and remained available through version 6 unix in version 7 unix 1979 the pwb shell was superseded by the bourne shell the pwb shell was also incorporated into some versions of programmer s workbench unix circa 1975 77 several features were introduced in the pwb shell remain in many later shells the if and goto commands were made internal to the shell and extended to allow if then else endif and switch and while constructs were introduced as well as onintr to ignore interrupts or catch them to perform cleanup simple variables could be used although their names were limited to one letter and some letters were reserved for special purposes of which some are the precursors of the environment variables found in all unix systems from version 7 onward for example the s variable was the ancestor of home used to avoid hard coding pathnames the p variable was the ancestor of path which let users search for commands in their own choice of directories unlike most of the unix systems
