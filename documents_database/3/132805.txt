the shares of the mint are held in trust for the crown in right of canada the mint produces all of canada s circulation coins and manufactures circulation coins on behalf of other nations the mint also designs and manufactures precious and base metal collector coins gold silver palladium and platinum bullion coins medals as well as medallions and tokens it further offers gold and silver refinery and assay services the mint serves the public s interest but is also mandated to operate in anticipation of profit i e to function in a commercial manner without relying on taxpayer support to fund its operations like private sector companies the mint has a board of directors consisting of a chair the president and ceo of the mint and eight other directors traditionally the president of the royal canadian mint is known as the master of the mint the president is marie lemay who was appointed to the position in 2018 the board of directors through the chair is accountable to the minister of finance the minister serves as the link between the mint cabinet and parliament the mint was named one of canada s top 100 employers by mediacorp canada inc
