his older brothers were fran ois ii duke of longueville and louis i d orl ans duke of longueville he was a relative of louis xi of france and louis xii of france the latter of whom provided for his education he began his ecclesiastical career as a cleric at chartres cathedral on november 29 1503 he was elected archbishop of toulouse he was the administrator of the archdiocese until he reached the canonical age of 27 he received the pallium on august 14 1517 and then occupied the see until his death on june 26 1521 he was named bishop of orl ans he also occupied that see until his death pope clement vii made him a cardinal priest in the consistory of march 3 1533 he received the red hat and the titular church of san martino ai monti at that time in 1533 he was traveling to marseille to witness pope clement vii perform the marriage of henry duke of orl ans and catherine de medici he died on the way to marseille at tarascon on september 24 1533
