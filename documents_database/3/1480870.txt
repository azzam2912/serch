howard the birthday party this album followed a whole decade of insecurity regarding his own singing his earlier release popular classical featured robert wyatt on vocals and passionate record collecting the latter is evident in typeface and colours on the cover of the album as well as the lyrics to sad song alluding to the beach boys album pet sounds and its song caroline no respectively the album was re released by troubadour easy action on cd in november 2015 remastered with new and extensive sleeve notes and with a bonus cd including work in progress and early versions of the songs on the album spanish label mapache also issued the album on vinyl all tracks composed and arranged by epic soundtracks
