piso became one of the founders of tropical medicine a minor planet 11240 piso and the plant genus pisonia are named for him piso finished his studies in caen in 1633 and settled in amsterdam as a doctor before leaving for brazil as a private physician and in the company of the painters albert eckhout and frans post there he recommended the consumption of fresh fish vegetables and fruits after discovering that soldiers and seamen suffered from physical problems resulting from malnutrition together with georg marcgrave and originally published by joannes de laet piso wrote the historia naturalis brasiliae 1648 an important early western insight into brazilian flora and fauna according to reports in the book besides studying tropical diseases and indigenous therapies including the use of ipecacuanha root and leaves of the jaborandi piso collected plants and animals in brazil
