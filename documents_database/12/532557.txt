august 24 1906 june 24 1980 was a cinematographer and the younger brother of filmmakers dziga vertov and mikhail kaufman kaufman was born into a family of jewish intellectuals in bia ystok when congress poland was part of the russian empire after the bolshevik revolution of 1917 poland regained its independence and boris moved there with his parents mikhail and denis better known as dziga vertov stayed in the soviet union and became important filmmakers producing avant garde and agitprop films the brothers later stayed in touch primarily by letters vertov visited boris kaufman in paris twice in 1929 and 1931 after graduating from the university of paris kaufman turned to cinematography collaborating with jean vigo and dimitri kirsanoff during world war ii he served in the french army against the nazis when france fell kaufman escaped to canada after working briefly with john grierson for the national film board of canada he moved to the united states in 1942 kaufman supported himself by filming short subjects and documentaries until director elia kazan chose him as director of photography for on the waterfront 1954 kaufman s first american feature film for which he won an academy award for best cinematography
