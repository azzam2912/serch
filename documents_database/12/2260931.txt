after two years performing in nelson henriquez s group 1972 1973 he decided to branch off and form his own pastor l pez y su combo many of the cumbias he has recorded are interpretations of works associated with peruvian cumbia including cari ito sin mi bonita y mentirosa and traicionera among others the success of his more than 30 year musical career has not been limited to his two nations of residence venezuela and colombia but spans the united states canada spain the uk and other countries with significant colombian and latino populations he was one of the foremost recording stars for the dominantly cumbia record label discos fuentes he died on 5 april 2019 at the age of 74 years at the north clinic in the city of cucuta colombia after having suffered a stroke
