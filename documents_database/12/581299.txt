shantaram or shantaram bapu was an indian marathi filmmaker film producer and actor he is most known for films such as dr kotnis ki amar kahani 1946 amar bhoopali 1951 jhanak jhanak payal baaje 1955 do aankhen barah haath 1957 navrang 1959 duniya na mane 1937 pinjara 1972 chani iye marathiche nagari and zunj v shantaram started his film career doing odd jobs in maharashtra film co owned by baburao painter at kolhapur he went on to debut as an actor in the silent film surekha haran in 1921 shantaram fondly known as annasaheb had an illustrious career as a filmmaker for almost six decades he was one of the early filmmakers to realize the efficacy of the film medium as an instrument of social change and used it successfully to advocate humanism on one hand and expose bigotry and injustice on the other v shantaram had a very keen interest in music it is said that he ghost wrote music for many of his music directors and took a very active part in the creation of music some of his songs had to rehearsed several times before they were approved by v shantaram he directed his first film netaji
