tyson born december 19 1924 is an american actress and former fashion model in a career spanning more than six decades she became known for her portrayal of strong african american women tyson is the recipient of three primetime emmy awards four black reel awards one screen actor guild award one tony award and an honorary academy award having appeared in minor film and television roles early in her career tyson garnered widespread attention and critical acclaim for her performance as rebecca morgan in sounder 1972 she was nominated for both the academy award and golden globe award for best actress for her work in the film tyson s portrayal of the title role in the 1974 television film the autobiography of miss jane pittman won her further praise among other accolades the role won her two emmy awards and a nomination for a bafta award for best actress in a leading role tyson has continued to act in film and on television in the 21st century in 2011 she played the role of constantine jefferson in the award winning film the help she has also played the role of ophelia harkness in american broadcasting company s legal drama how
