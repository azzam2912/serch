a traditional highlead or gravity system will function with just two cables a skidding line as the names imply the skidding line is used to drag the logs in and the haulback line is used to drag the skidding line back out for the next turn or group of logs a skyline system will add a third line the skyline whose function is to hold the skidding line and the haulback line off the ground or in the sky the yarder itself is located on a landing a flat area on top of the ridge that is being logged after the trees are retrieved by the yarder the limbs are bucked removed and the logs are then placed in piles awaiting transport
