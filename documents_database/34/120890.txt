j b sumner built the first general store and post office in the town that was later named for him in 1885 where he became the first postmaster he donated the land for the right of way and park to the railroad company in 1888 when sumner incorporated in 1900 j b sumner was elected the first mayor he donated the lots for the first jail and the courthouse built in 1902 he taught his own children and the children of his tenants at a school located between sumner and webb mississippi sumner later moved to bentonville arkansas with his daughter before moving to mississippi sumner had served in the 22nd al volunteer infantry company a in the confederate states of america army and was wounded during the operations before the battle of stones river near murfreesboro tennessee the evening of december 31 1862 sumner is buried in his family cemetery indian mound cemetery
