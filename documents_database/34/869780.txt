the stems of cue notes all go in the same direction and cue notes are transposed into the key of the part entering there are also passages in certain scores where there is an indication above a line of notes saying cue notes or simply cue but this indication does not have anything to do with the cue notes explained above opposed to these these cue notes are more like alternative notes which can either be played or not played depending on the conductor s decision this practice is particularly common in recordings of orchestral film music where they often like to try out several orchestral colors and weightings in just one recording session and therefore use cued passages to try out other options
