the games were first held in 1969 in saipan northern mariana islands the 2010 micronesian games were initially due to be held in majuro marshall islands until the hosts withdrew the 2010 games were hosted by palau the federated states of micronesia won the bidding to host the 2014 micronesian games in pohnpei state and later won again against cnmi for the 2018 micronesian games to be held in yap state after the inaugural 1969 edition the games were supposed to be a regular event however the second edition did not take place until 1990 since then the games have been held every four years without fail participants include four sovereign countries the marshall islands kiribati nauru and palau a commonwealth in political union with the united states the northern mariana islands an organized unincorporated territory of the united states guam and the four constituent states of the federated states of micronesia chuuk pohnpei kosrae and yap which compete separately from one another these ten countries states and territories are all located within the micronesian region of oceania all participants also take part in the pacific games although the federated states of micronesia competes as a unified country there athletes
