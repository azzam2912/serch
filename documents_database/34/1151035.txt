access to the commune is by the d926 road from gomont in the north east which passes through the village and continues south west to asfeld the commune is mixed forest and farmland the aisne river passes through the centre of the commune flowing from north east to south west on its way to join the oise at compi gne the parallel canal des ardennes passes through the southern tip of the commune the name balham comes from the name of a person ballo heim it was balaan around 1172 and balehan in 1211 1212 list of successive mayors in 2010 the commune had 135 inhabitants the evolution of the number of inhabitants is known from the population censuses conducted in the commune since 1793 from the 21st century a census of communes with fewer than 10 000 inhabitants is held every five years unlike larger communes that have a sample survey every year
