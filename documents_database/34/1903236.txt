the format similar to dutch television series soundmixshow was first used in spain under the title in 2011 the show challenges celebrities to perform as different iconic music artists every week which are chosen by the show s randomiser they are then judged by the panel of celebrity judges each celebrity becomes transformed into a different singer each week and performs an iconic song and dance routine well known by that particular singer the randomiser can choose any older or younger artist available in the machine or even a singer of the opposite sex or a deceased singer the contestants are awarded points from the judges based on their singing and dance routines after the jury vote the contestants have to give a set of points to a fellow contestant of their choice the total score of each contestant is counted by summing the points from judges and contestant s voting in case of a tie the judges will choose the weeks winner whoever is at the top of the leaderboard at the end of the each show receives a cash prize for a charity of their choice and a further grand prize for the series champion since the format
