the film tells the story of yuriko a girl from hiroshima who comes to moscow to study ballet the film s title is a reference to the alain resnais film hiroshima my love yuriko ono is a young promising dancer in japan yuriko s opportunity to become a professional dancer comes when she is invited to moscow to study ballet at the bolshoi theatre yukiko finds happiness when she falls in love with a muscovite sculptor and wins the competition of the bolshoi theatre graduates but her happiness is short a diagnosis of blood cancer abruptly impedes her path of dedication to art and seems to plunge her life into a storm the unfortunate girl was born in the capital city of hiroshima which suffered one of the two atomic bombs from the us army in 1945 after days of struggling with the legacy of the terrible consequence of the battle yukiko s last breath is in a hospital in the city of moscow far from home and loved ones she dies in the arms of a teary eyed russian boy in whom she entrusted her heart
