to be included in this list the formats must all have been used in the field or for test shooting and they must all use photochemical images that are formed or projected on a film base a transparent substrate which supports the photosensitive emulsion as well the formats must have been used to make more than just a few test frames the camera must be fast enough in frames per second to create an illusion of motion consistent with the persistence of vision phenomenon the format must be significantly unique from other listed formats in regard to its image capture or image projection the format characteristics should be clearly definable in several listed parameters e g film gauge aspect ratio etc formats are listed in chronological order and by release date in the case of multiple formats within one year if this can be determined undated formats are listed at the bottom in alphabetical order
