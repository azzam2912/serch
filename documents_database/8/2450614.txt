it is an mci recognised medical college established in 2013 under the patronage of the esi corporation a central autonomous body under ministry of labour and employment government of india the institute is under the administrative control of the esi corporation it is affiliated to rajiv gandhi university of health sciences bangalore the college is attached to a 500 bedded multi speciality hospital which was built in 2013 but became operational in 2014 the campus is spread in over 75 acres and provide primary and tertiary healthcare facilities to the workers insured under the esi corporation as well as non insured persons and is one of the tertiary care referral center for smaller esi clinic and dispensaries the following courses are offered for 100 seats in mbbs course neet ug entrance exam is held every year in which college offers15 seats via all india quota 50 seats via the state quota and 35 via the ip quota
