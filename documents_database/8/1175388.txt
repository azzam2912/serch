the hat creek basin includes a broad upper plateau area encircled by the gentle but high summits of the clear range and to its east the cornwall hills this area is known as upper hat creek adjacent to upper hat creek is the gateway to marble canyon and a rancherie of the pavilion first nation who are both a st at imc and secwepemc people during the fraser canyon and cariboo gold rushes an important trail northwards from the lower fraser canyon led from foster bar on the fraser via laluwissen creek into upper hat creek then via the creek to the bonaparte river the economy of the basin is ranching based and includes some of the oldest ranches in the british columbia interior on the northwest edge of the upper hat creek basin there is a large lignite deposit and several exploratory pits some dating back to the 19th century but some more recent part of an intended but now shelved at least temporarily hat creek coal thermal proposal
