she grew up in ypres belgium her talent was discovered by th odore ceriez 1831 1904 a painter who had married her sister h l ne since women were not yet admitted to the academies he educated her and already in 1885 her painting de oesters the oysters was exhibited at a salon in spa jules breton saw her work and encouraged her to go to paris for further education from 1887 to 1891 de hem mostly lived in paris where alfred stevens was one of her teachers at the private acad mie julian she painted living and sometimes nude models which was at that time in belgium still forbidden for women in 1891 she returned to ypres where she shared a studio with ceriez at the g de stuersstraat 33 she soon received commissions for portraits and also created still lives in paris she frequently exhibited at the salon de la societ des artistes fran ais de hem exhibited her work at the palace of fine arts and the woman s building at the 1893 world s columbian exposition in chicago illinois in 1900 she was the only belgian painter to exhibit at the women s exhibition in london
