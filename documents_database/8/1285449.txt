norelf ltd or the santa clara 1996 a c 800 1996 3 w l r 105 1996 3 all e r 193 is an english contract law case about the effect of non performance in accepting a contracting partner s repudiatory breach of contract norelf ltd contracted to sell a cargo of propane to vitol sa in 1991 the propane market had been very volatile the cargo was being shipped on the santa clara from houston u s it was meant to leave before march 7 on march 8 while it was still being loaded vitol sent a telex to norelf saying it did not wish the contract to continue because it was not going to arrive on time i e vitol repudiated the contract the ship was loaded and it sailed on march 9 the price of the cargo fell neither side did anything further to perform the contract norelf sold the cargo at a loss and then claimed damages 950 000 from vitol for breach of contract the arbitrator held that vitol s telex was an anticipatory breach of contract but norelf s failure to take further steps to perform the contract was sufficient communication to vitol that
