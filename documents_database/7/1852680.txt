the tournament began on november 14 2012 and ended on december 12 2012 the winners and runners up of the competition qualified for the 2013 14 concacaf champions league the eight best teams in the general table qualified for the competition the eight qualified teams play two games against each other on a home and away basis the winner of each match up is determined by aggregate score the teams were seeded one to eight in quarterfinals and will be re seeded one to four in semifinals depending on their position in the general table the higher seeded teams play on their home field during the second leg the first legs of the quarterfinals were played on november 14 and 15 the second legs were played on november 17 and 18 kickoffs are given in local time utc 6 unless stated otherwise the first legs of the semifinals were played on november 22 and november 25 the second legs were played on december 3 and 4 kickoffs are given in local time utc 6 unless stated otherwise the first leg of the final was played on november 29 the second leg on december 2 kickoffs are given in local time
