elphick was born on 4 august 1921 in country victoria foster south gippsland and completed her high school education and nursing training in melbourne she entered the novitiate of the sisters of charity on 2 july 1943 and was professed on 14 january 1946 her first appointment was in 1946 at st vincent s hospital in sydney she proceeded through a number of positions at various st vincent s hospitals around the country from 1957 1962 she served as the mother rectress in launceston and from 1963 she served as the mother rectress in sydney managing the hospital as well as the convent in recognition of her service she was appointed an officer of the order of the british empire obe in 1977 a member of the order of australia am in 1986 and a companion of the order of australia ac in 2006 in 2002 a fund raising rose for the victor chang institute was named after her in 2004 a wing at the st vincent s hospital in darlinghurst was named in her honour she retired in 1997 and died at the st joseph s aged care facility kensington sydney on 20 june 2008
