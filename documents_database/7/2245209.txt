the production features a line up of magicians who each specialise in different magical disciplines supported by a team of assistants the show was created by producer jamie hendry and directed by lloyd wood and anthony owen with original music by ryan martin and michael bradley the production made its world premiere at the no l coward theatre in london swest end on 24 july 2015 in june 2015 it was announced that the cast would include jonathan goodwin escapologist lu s de matos jamie allan magician ali cook chris cox magician ben hart magician and katherine mills the production received mostly positive reviews with the daily mail calling it the hottest act in town in november 2015 it was announced that the production would tour the uk opening at the mayflower theatre southampton on 3 february 2016 a major cast change took place with josephine lee magical bones and lee thompson joining the cast with lu s de matos ali cook jamie allan magician and aaron crow all leaving a production opened on 5 may 2016 at the forum de beyrouth in beirut in may 2016 it was announced that the production would return to london s west end
