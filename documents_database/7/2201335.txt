he served as president of the college of the holy cross from 1887 to 1889 cahill was born in ireland on 24 july 1844 but moved to the united states with his parents as a young child he entered the society of jesus in 1868 at frederick maryland he studied philosophy and theology at woodstock college and was ordained to the priesthood by archbishop james gibbons in 1880 cahill spent his early priesthood serving in parishes including st ignatius church in baltimore cahill held a series of administrative posts at loyola college now loyola university maryland saint joseph s college now saint joseph s university fordham university and boston college in 1887 he was appointed president of the college of the holy cross but resigned only two years later due to ill health he went for health purposes to colorado and new mexico where he worked as a laborer until 1897 in 1905 he was appointed vice president of georgetown university while at the same time working at holy trinity church in washington d c his last appointment was pastor of st joseph s church in philadelphia pennsylvania where he died on 25 october 1910
