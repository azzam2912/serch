freeform primarily broadcasts programming geared toward teenagers and young adults with some programming skewing toward young women in the 14 34 age range a target demographic designated by the channel as becomers its programming includes contemporary off network syndicated reruns and original series feature films and made for tv original movies since the network was launched on april 29 1977 it has undergone various changes to its programming format and naming under its four different owners the network was founded as a religious channel the cbn satellite service an extension of televangelist pat robertson s christian broadcasting network it evolved into a family focused entertainment network in 1981 and was spun off into a for profit company known as international family entertainment ife in 1990 eventually becoming known as the family channel as a condition of the spin off the channel was contractually required to maintain airings of the 700 club and an annual telethon on the last sunday in january these conditions have applied to all future owners of the channel in 1997 ife and the family channel were acquired by a joint venture between news corporation and saban entertainment resulting in its re branding as the fox family
