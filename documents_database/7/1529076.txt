bugge graduated from trondhjem cathedral school in 1823 and from the royal frederick university with the cand philol degree in 1829 in september 1831 in christiania he married anne marie magelssen 1811 1874 their son wilhelm bugge became a bishop bugge was also uncle of johannes christian piene and great grandfather of leif vetlesen he was hired as principal at trondhjem cathedral school in 1833 he was a school reformer and first published his thoughts on the school system in 1835 s he was sent by the norwegian state to study schools in france and german states from 1836 to 1837 in 1838 he finished his reflections from the travel in three volumes named the work was published by the norwegian state the next year and even awarded with a gold medal by frederick augustus ii of saxony in 1839 bugge got a seat on the public commission he had ideas that bore stems of the comprehensive school thought he was inspired by grundtvig s idea about educating the masses and also supported ivar aasen s endeavors however he also wanted to protect the learned schools which taught classical subjects from too much influence from natural sciences and modern languages
