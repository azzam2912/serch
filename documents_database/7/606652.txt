was a football club based in the castle vale area of birmingham england the club was previously known as king s heath and castle vale king s heath before adopting its final name in 2005 the club resigned as members of the midland combination premier division in july 2012 and folded the club was founded in 1964 as the pub team of the horseshoe pub in the king s heath area of birmingham the team played in the south birmingham league and mercian league for a number of years in 1977 they changed their name to kings heath f c and joined the midland football combination initially in division two and in 1982 83 were promoted to the top division which was renamed the premier division in the same year by this stage the club although still named kings heath was based at the glen in solihull but were forced to leave the ground when midway through an extensive programme of ground improvements their financial backer abruptly pulled out leaving them unable to complete the work the team was able to groundshare with knowle and later shirley town but the facilities at these grounds did not meet premier division requirements
