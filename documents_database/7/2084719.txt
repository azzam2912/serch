it s located in the south zone of m rida municipality between 20 47 and 20 55 of north latitude and 89 33 and 89 40 of west longitude the reserve limits on the north with the city of m rida south with the commissariat of yaxnic and tex n c mara east with the municipality of kanas n and west with the commissariat of san jos tzal and ticimul it provides 50 of the water in the city of m rida and is home to 168 of bird species most of them migratory as well as plants mammals reptiles invertebrates and amphibians it contains 7 historic haciendas archaeological sites cenotes as well as a nature preservation and the biological sciences campus for uady the zone subject to ecological conservation cuxtal reserve is recognized as the first protected natural area anp by its initials in spanish promoted and decreed directly by a municipality in the state of yucatan during the m rida city council 1991 1993 its establishment as anp was approved by the council on june 28 1993 and its decree was published in the official gazette of the government of the state of yucatan on july 14 1993
