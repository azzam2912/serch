the film tells the story of el chuncho a bandit and bill tate or el ni o who is a counter revolutionary in mexico chuncho soon learns that social revolution is more important than mere money during the mexican revolution a durango bound government munitions train is forced to stop due to the presence of a crucified army officer on the tracks el chuncho mu os a gun runner loyal to the revolutionary leader general el as leads his gang in an assault on the train lieutenant alvaro ferreira attempts to save the officer but upon being fatally wounded by chuncho he is forced to order the train to run the officer over and escape the bandits bill tate an american passenger on the train kills the engineer and stops the train again allowing chuncho and his gang to kill the remaining soldiers and take their weapons posing as a former prisoner of the army tate joins the gang and is quickly befriended by chuncho who nicknames him ni o after several heists the gang travels to the town of san miguel where chuncho meets with his old friend raimundo to overthrow the weakhearted town boss don felipe rosaria felipe
