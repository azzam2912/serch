he represented the electoral district of pictou centre in the nova scotia house of assembly from 1970 to 1974 he is a member of the nova scotia liberal party fiske was born in clarence nova scotia and graduated from high school in annapolis nova scotia growing up on a wood mill run by his father he began his business career working in the bank later to become an entrepreneur businessman restaurateur and motel hotel owner operator fiske entered provincial politics in the 1970 election defeating progressive conservative incumbent donald r macleod by 434 votes in pictou centre on october 28 1970 fiske was appointed to the executive council of nova scotia as provincial secretary and minister of trade and industry on july 15 1971 gerald regan shuffled his cabinet moving fiske to minister of development he remained as provincial secretary until another shuffle in november 1971 fiske resigned from cabinet prior to an august 1973 cabinet shuffle and did not seek re election in 1974 he later served as chairman of the nova scotia gaming commission although forced to resign due to indiscretions between the halifax casino and the government at the time his claims were found to be truthful
