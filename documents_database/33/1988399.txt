r martin and its television adaptation game of thrones olenna is first mentioned in a game of thrones 1996 and appears in a storm of swords 2000 and a feast for crows 2005 she is the matriarch of the powerful house tyrell the largest and second wealthiest of the eight great houses of westeros olenna is characterized by her cunning ambition and sharp wit the latter of which being the foundation for her title the queen of thorns alongside the tyrell sigil of a rose although her family is allied with the lannisters in king s landing she often finds her own machinations at odds with theirs especially those of tywin lannister she along with petyr baelish is responsible for the death of king joffrey baratheon during his wedding to her granddaughter and prot g margaery olenna is portrayed by veteran english actress diana rigg in the hbo television adaptation who has received significant critical praise for her portrayal rigg has received emmy award nominations for outstanding guest actress in a drama series for her performances in 2013 2014 2015 and 2018 olenna tyrell also known as the queen of thorns is a former redwyne and the mother of mace
