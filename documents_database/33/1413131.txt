calvo was a chilean landowner a former patriot who had switched sides to the royalists during the siege of chillan the previous year manuel blanco encalada arrived in quechereguas where he received a note from ngel calvo proposing a site for the forthcoming battle on the following day manuel blanco encalada took up position and launched his attack the battle seemed initially to be successful but royalist reinforcements rapidly forced him to retreat until he reached cancha rayada where he finally confronted the royalist advance the patriot forces collapsed and a large number of officials and several unit commanders fled back towards santiago patriot commanders tried to regroup but were taken prisoner along with many of their soldiers in a quarter of an hour 450 royalists had defeated 1 400 patriotic soldiers taking 300 prisoners food ammunition and guns
