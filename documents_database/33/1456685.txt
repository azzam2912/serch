the redactor is also theorized to have made other editorial changes to the gospel the gospel of john is oft viewed as having docetic implications the redactor therefore is theorized to have inserted more directly reference to jesus s humanity and the material nature of his flesh thus bringing the work in line with proto orthodox theology the gospel of john generally presents the point of view of realized eschatology that the kingdom of heaven was established by jesus s resurrection the redactor is theorized to have inserted elements of future eschatology the view that a future kingdom of heaven has yet to be realized but will at a point in the future the gospel of john generally presents peter as secondary to the disciple whom jesus loved the redactor is theorized to have altered the gospel so as to reassert peterine supremacy among the apostles
