the screenplay was written by paul webb the film follows the events leading up to and during the selma to montgomery marches and the resulting establishment of the voting rights act of 1965 which prohibited racial discrimination in voting in the united states the film stars david oyelowo as civil rights activist martin luther king jr tom wilkinson as president lyndon b johnson and tim roth as governor of alabama george wallace carmen ejogo dylan baker wendell pierce common and winfrey feature in supporting roles selma premiered at the afi fest on november 11 2014 in los angeles paramount pictures initially provided the film a limited release on december 25 before a wide release at over 2 100 theaters on january 9 2015 selma grossed a worldwide total of over 66 million on a production budget of 20 million rotten tomatoes a review aggregator surveyed 226 reviews and judged 99 percent to be positive as of june 2016 the film garnered awards and nominations in a variety of categories with particular praise for its direction oyelowo s portrayal of king and the song glory by john legend and common at the 87th academy awards selma won for best original song
