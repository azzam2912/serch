the queen was represented in malawi by a governor general malawi became a republic under the constitution of 1966 and the monarch and governor general were replaced by an executive president the succession to the throne was the same as the succession to the british throne the governor general was the representative of the monarch in malawi and exercised most of the powers of the monarch the governor general was appointed for an indefinite term serving at the pleasure of the monarch after the passage of the statute of westminster 1931 the governor general was appointed solely on the advice of the cabinet of malawi without the involvement of the british government in the event of a vacancy the chief justice served as officer administering the government under the country s 1966 1994 and 1995 constitutions the president is executive head of state the first president was elected by the national assembly but later presidents were elected in direct popular elections for a five year term in the event of a vacancy the vice president becomes president there are three living former malawian heads of state
