fatoumata nafo traor is a malian medical doctor and public health official she is the regional director africa at the international federation of red cross and red crescent society ifrc prior to this role she served as the executive director of roll back malaria partnership she worked as a health specialist at the world bank in bamako and served as minister of health and later on minister of social affairs solidarity and the elderly of mali from 2000 to 2002 she served as chair of the assembly of ministers of ecowas and was member of the board of the global alliance for vaccine and immunization gavi nafo traor was executive secretary of the roll back malaria partnership secretariat from 2003 to 2004 and the director of the who roll back malaria department from 2004 to 2005 and then as who representative to the congo and ethiopia she is a member of professional associations and has authored technical publications and studies nafo traor is currently a board member of the global fund to fight aids tuberculosis and malaria innovative vector control consortium and medicines for malaria venture nafo traor was twice awarded the national distinction of chevalier and officer de l
