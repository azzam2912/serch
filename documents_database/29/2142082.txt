hopin academy derived its name from its educational philosophy as a school designed for students to hop in and hop out rather than having strictly organized timetable structures the school specializes in training online marketing social media communication writing video production entrepreneurial work and web communications hopin academy has established an international academic partnership with bryn mawr college and haverford college through the bico dalun summer action research fellowship a learning program fostered in 2010 where bryn mawr and haverford students create community projects with ghanaian partners according to the hopin academy website they offers courses in writing design film and entrepreneurial work they also provides training to ngos government organizations and other schools such as the olive school of journalism hopin academy was started in 2010 as a social enterprise that aims to be an alternative to the ghanaian public education system the educational initiative was co founded by a ghanaian entrepreneur maccarthy m mac gbathy and the danish film maker and harvard trained innovator anders midtgaard in addition to offering courses and technological resources hopin academy supports barcamp tamale which is a networking forum hosted in tamale that connects ghanaian leaders and innovators with young people hopin academy
