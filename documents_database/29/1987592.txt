the trail is 12 km long and connects rengsdorf via strassenhaus with h mmerich leading through the westerwald forest the butterpfad dates back to the time that farmers of the region transported their goods mainly butter and milk via this route to neuwied where they sold their goods at the market the trail is marked with red white waymarks and along the trail information tables are located providing background information on the history of this activity in earlier times the trail lead not only from h mmerich to rengsdorf but continued through the alte scheid to oberbieber and from there by tram to the market in neuwied the trail connects not only rengsdorf with h mmerich but also the westerwaldsteig in obersteinebach via a 4 2 km long path from h mmerich with the rheinsteig and the klosterweg in rengsdorf from its starting point at the st kastor chapel in rengsdorf the trail follows the v lkerwiesenbach valley through beech grove to the hut philippsruhe at the kreisstra e 105 crossing this street the trail passes bonefeld in the direction of strassenhaus there is the option to follow a small detour to the alteburg bonefeld a monument of the middle
