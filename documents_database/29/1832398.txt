each box set is made up of four hour long stories which also tell an overall story the first part of dark eyes the great war won the 2014 bbc audio drama award for best online or non broadcast drama the cover and promotional photographs for dark eyes are the first to not feature the eighth doctor in the clothes or variations thereof he wore in his original television appearance in the 1996 tv movie the new outfit was premiered in 2010 at the armageddon pop culture expo along with a redesigned sonic screwdriver courtesy of weta workshop these changes were independently produced and were not affiliated with big finish productions or the bbc but were accepted as suitable for official use after the fact three sequel series have been released the first dark eyes 2 was released in february 2014 dark eyes 3 and 4 came out in november 2014 and march 2015 respectively
