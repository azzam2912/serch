he studied sculpture at the ecole des beaux arts in paris under p j cavelier louis ernest barrias and andr allar and frequently exhibited his work at the salon des artistes fran ais between 1882 and 1924 winning several awards his work spanned designs for busts statuettes and bas relief panels for which he obtained many public commissions these included decorative groups for the zurich bank for the cairo museum the facade of the royal automobile club in london and many buildings in paris among the latter was a figure of abundance for the ritz and the 1906 bas reliefs of the seasons for madame h riot s town house his native marseilles has little of his work except for a baroque virgin and child at the corner of the rue fontange and the rue blanqui among the influences on him at the start of the 20th century was art nouveau as can be seen in the sinuous lines of his gilt bronze jardini re modelled with nudes and mermaids emerging from waves of 1900 or of the marble statue of jean de la fontaine s the cat changed into a woman of 1906 that he later cast in bronze
