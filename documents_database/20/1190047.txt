ms varrenti has also appeared in several television serials as a radio broadcaster she has worked for community radio 3rrr abc melbourne and abc radio national in 2008 penguin books published her memoir this is not my beautiful life and a short story in the anthology how we met affirm press published ms varrenti s story seriously funny in the anthology of women s writing she s having a laugh in 2015 as a freelance writer and columnist she has contributed to the age the australian the australian women s weekly mamamia hoopla daily life the good weekend the australian education union the big issue and daily review she was a theatre critic for the melbourne times from 2007 to 2009 for the age from 2011 to 2012 and a book critic for the australian and the age in 2016 and 2017 she taught creative writing at deakin university in 2014 and at the university of melbourne in 2015 2016 2017 and 2018
