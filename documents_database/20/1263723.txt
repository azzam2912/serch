the series was a spin off from the sitcom pardon the expression itself a spin off from the highly popular soap opera coronation street leonard swindley played by arthur lowe was the central character along with wally hunt played by robert dorning swindley was formerly the manager of the fashion retail store gamma garments in coronation street and the deputy manager of the department store dobson and hawks in pardon the expression in this series he becomes a professional speaker on astrology who encounters various supernatural events on his travels around the country along with his colleague wally hunt after they were both fired from dobson and hawks in the final episode of pardon the expression the series directors were david boisseau and michael cox production designers were dennis parkin and roy stonehouse the series was not recorded in front of a studio audience and had no laughter track
