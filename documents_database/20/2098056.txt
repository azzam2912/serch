he studied theology in yale theological seminary for three years after leaving college and was ordained pastor of the congregational church in scotland conn march 20 1844 from this charge he was dismissed june 26 1861 from july 1861 to nov 1863 he supplied the pulpit of the congregational church in groton conn in 1864 he removed to thompson conn and there resided until his death in the interval preaching in westminster in 1864 65 and in east putnam from april 1868 to nov 1869 he was a member of the connecticut state house of representatives in the sessions of 1866 and 1867 he died after great sufferings oct 9th 1872 from the effects of a cartilaginous tumor which had been forming in the abdomen for more than three years tallman was married may 17 1842 to frances m daughter of simon hazleton of haddam she died july 30 1860 he was again married april 27 1864 to hannah c graves of thompson who survived him his children were a son and a daughter by his first and a son and a daughter by his second marriage the elder son graduated yale in 1867
