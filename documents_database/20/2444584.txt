harmon harmon was the founder of the afro american cultural foundation in 1969 and the monument is a legacy of that institution an engraved plaque recognizes the valiant efforts of a revolutionary war unit composed largely of black soldiers that fought on may 13 1782 under the command of col christopher greene while the patriotism of two white soldiers and a guide killed in the deadly skirmish known as the battle of pine s bridge had been commemorated earlier with a plaque at the site in 1900 the deaths and bravery of more than 40 men defending america s freedom had been overlooked the monument was added to the african american heritage trail of westchester county in 2004 as part of a mission to preserve and interpret the legacy and contributions that people of african descent have made to the development of our unique american identity it is one of only 13 such sites it was sponsored by the afro american cultural foundation of westchester county together with the rhode island black heritage society
