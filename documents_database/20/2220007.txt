built in 1915 it is a rare early 20th century example of a warren pony truss bridge it was listed on the national register of historic places in 2006 as bridge no 27 lover s lane is a short east west road located in southern berlin joining chandler road to the west and vermont route 12 to the east each of which run roughly parallel to the north flowing dog river the bridge across the river is oriented roughly northeast southwest at a point where the river briefly bends to the west it is a single span warren pony truss structure in length resting on modern concrete abutments the truss elements are made of rolled steel joined by bolts a replacement for original rivets the bridge floor is also a modern replacement consisting of rolled steel flange beams supporting a wooden deck the bridge was built about 1918 replacing an earlier wooden structure probably an open wooden truss bridge it was built at a time when vermont s bridge construction including local bridges such as this one was becoming increasingly influenced by professional engineers hired by the state s department of transportation this bridge is one of a shrinking number
