seridis was bornt in 1850s or 60s in flampouro of florina he was a wood merchant acting in central and west macedonia he started his armed actions early even before the arrival of the official officers from the greek state against bulgarian komitadjis of giannitsa and goumenissa in 1904 he met with konstantinos mazarakis ainian who helped him form an eighty member body of macedonians k mazarakis entrusted him with the leadership of the body despite his advanced age as he was experienced in wars and respected by the soldiers at the beginning of 1905 his body acted in the regions of pelagonia and monastiri now bitola and in the february of that year he succeeded in beating a komitadji group near kru evo however his main area of action remained the area of goumenissa and mount paiko where he restricted the activity of the bulgarian armed groups including apostol petkov s his presence and successful operations resulted in raising the morale of the greeks of the region captain spanos and his group were arrested by an ottoman army detachment near goumenissa resulting in the end of his actions
