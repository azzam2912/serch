the text is divided into three books each with aphoristic teachings on virtue aram dharma wealth porul artha and love inbam kama considered one of the great works on ethics and morality it is known for its universality and secular nature its authorship is traditionally attributed to valluvar also known in full as thiruvalluvar the text has been dated variously from 300 bce to 5th century ce the traditional accounts describe it as the last work of the third sangam but linguistic analysis suggests a later date of 450 to 500 ce and that it was composed after the sangam period the kural is traditionally praised with epithets and alternate titles such as the tamil veda and the divine book it emphasizes non violence and moral vegetarianism as virtues for an individual in addition it highlights truthfulness self restraint gratitude hospitality kindness goodness of wife duty giving and so forth besides covering a wide range of social and political topics such as king ministers taxes justice forts war greatness of army and soldier s honor death sentence for the wicked agriculture education abstinence from alcohol and intoxicants it also includes chapters on friendship love sexual union and domestic life the
