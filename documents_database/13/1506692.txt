all 75 seats in the parliament were up for election the parliament of galicia was the devolved unicameral legislature of the autonomous community of galicia having legislative power in regional matters as defined by the spanish constitution and the galician statute of autonomy as well as the ability to vote confidence in or withdraw it from a president of the regional government voting for the parliament was on the basis of universal suffrage which comprised all nationals over eighteen registered in galicia and in full enjoyment of their political rights the 75 members of the parliament of galicia were elected using the d hondt method and a closed list proportional representation with a threshold of 5 percent of valid votes which included blank ballots being applied in each constituency parties not reaching the threshold were not taken into consideration for seat distribution additionally the use of the d hondt method might result in an effective threshold over five percent depending on the district magnitude seats were allocated to constituencies corresponding to the provinces of la coru a lugo orense and pontevedra each constituency was entitled to an initial minimum of 10 seats with the remaining 35 allocated among the constituencies
