moro joined a childhood friend litto nebbia and three others in forming the pioneer argentine rock and roll band los gatos in 1966 the group became known for their all night performances and composed most of their own songs many in the well known neighborhood caf la perla del once facing plaza miserere one such composition la balsa the raft was written at that location by nebbia and the ill fated songwriter tanguito on may 2 1967 and following its release on the rca victor label on july 3 sold over 250 000 copies they were the first argentine rock group to achieve renown outside their country and their first albums became known as the birth of argentine rock los gatos split in 1970 however moro then joined ex almendra guitarist edelmiro molinari in color humano and in 1976 joined charly garc a keyboard and vocals in two bands la m quina de hacer p jaros and starting in 1978 ser gir n the most popular argentine rock band of their era following ser gir n dissolution in 1982 he played professionally with le n gieco and others exploring african rhythms with bassist beto satragni in a 1982 crossover album
