kowalczyk born september 27 1975 is an american musician he is the brother of ed kowalczyk the lead singer of the band live kowalczyk was born in york pennsylvania to a family of polish descent he began learning the guitar at age 12 while studying in the radio television and film program at philadelphia s temple university he began writing songs and performing with his first band portion portion recorded two extended plays and opened for live and other bands in 1999 he began playing as the touring rhythm guitarist for live a position which he held for 10 years he appeared as a guest musician on the live albums the distance to here and v and featured in the band s only concert dvd release live at the paradiso amsterdam during his time with live kowalczyk relocated to los angeles and formed the band adam the weight which released the album looking up from the ground in 2003 by 2005 he was living in lancaster pennsylvania and had recorded a solo ep the dream in january 2011 adam became lead singer of the lancaster based band the mint kowalczyk is married to samantha their daughters lola ivy and olivia
