today jpsm offers coursework on site and online offering ms and ph d degrees certificate programs and short courses on the subjects of survey methodology survey statistics and data science in the early 1990s the u s federal statistical system interagency council on statistical policy sought assistance to provide advanced training to their employees in survey statistics and methodology employees who had graduate degrees from traditional statistics programs lacked knowledge on the design of complex samples and developing survey instruments interdisciplinary training which was necessary for implementing large scale national surveys and censuses in order to accomplish this initiative the national science foundation set up a grant that would provide an initial five years of funding for a university to establish this program the grant was awarded to the joint program in survey methodology jpsm a group made up by the university of maryland college park the university of michigan and westat and was headquartered at the university of maryland college park academic courses began in fall 1993 under the heading of the first jpsm director sociologist stanley presser the second director of jpsm serving from 1996 to 2001 was robert groves who went on to later head the us
