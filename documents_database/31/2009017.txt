the signing of a coalition agreement between imro and nfsb marks the end of the bbt imro coalition the coalition agreement of the patriotic front was signed on 3 august 2014 and states its purpose to be for a revival of the bulgarian economy a fight against monopolies achieving modern education and healthcare and a fair and uncorrupt judiciary the members of the alliance are proud national ideal for unity middle european class association patriot undivided bulgaria national movement bg patriot union of the patriotic forces defense national association of alternate soldiery for the honor of epaulette national movement for the salvation of the fatherland and national democratic party the coalition s electoral debut was in the early 2014 parliamentary election
