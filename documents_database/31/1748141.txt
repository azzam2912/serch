the film is based on a novel of the same name lalu rosalind chao is a young chinese woman who is sold by her impoverished family and transported against her will to the american west in 1880 upon her arrival in california she meets jim dennis dun a chinese wife trader who sells her to hong king michael paul chan a successful chinese merchant who lives in an idaho mining town the two set off on the long journey to idaho and eventually strike up a friendship along the way when they finally arrive in the rough isolated town she is distraught to discover that she is not going to be hong king s wife instead she is to work in his saloon as his newest prostitute under a new name china polly she is further dismayed when jim abruptly disappears leaving her to fend for herself the following night when hong king tries to sell her virtue to the highest bidder lalu violently refuses to submit to her would be suitors and successfully avoids becoming a prostitute thanks in part to the intervention of a kind stranger charlie bemis chris cooper who turns out to be hong king s
