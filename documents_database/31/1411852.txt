a violent dispute erupted between migrant uyghurs and han chinese workers at a toy factory in shaoguan as a result of allegations of the sexual assault of a han chinese female groups of han chinese set upon uyghur co workers leading to at least two uyghurs being violently killed by angry han chinese men although other reports indicate a higher death toll and some 118 people injured most of them uyghurs the event was widely cited as the trigger event for july 2009 r mqi riots which ostensibly started as a peaceful street protest demanding official action over the two uyghurs who died in shaoguan following trials in october 2009 one person was executed and several others sentenced to terms between life imprisonment and five to seven years the factory where the incident took place is the xuri early light toy factory owned by hong kong based early light international holdings ltd the largest toy manufacturer in the world the company s shaoguan factory in the wujiang district employs some 16 000 workers at the behest of the guangdong authorities it hired 800 workers from kashgar in xinjiang as part of an ethnic program which relocated 200 000 young uyghurs
