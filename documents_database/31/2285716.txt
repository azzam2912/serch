the super series features 8 top rated super middleweight boxers in a single elimination tournament its winner will hold the wba s world title as well as the muhammad ali trophy the tournament was organized by comosa ag a cruiserweight tournament is held concurrently with prize money for both competitions totalling us 50 million the format of the tournament required 4 seeded fighters to each choose their opponent from the 4 remaining unseeded fighters in a draft the seeded fighters were determined by the organizers the draft took place on july 8 2017 in a gala hosted by actress melanie winiger in monte carlo chris eubank chose the opponent for the winner of the eubank jr vs abraham bout the first wbss super middleweight match featured callum smith and erik skoglund two young undefeated fighters competing for a spot in the semifinals at the official weigh in smith hit the scales at 167 3 5 lbs while skoglund weighed in around 167 3 5 lbs the fight took place at echo arena in liverpool and was announced by jimmy lennon jr smith beat skoglund by unanimous decision to win one of four quarter finals in front of his home crowd
