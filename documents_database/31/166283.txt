he twice served as the eighth premier of quebec taillon s first term of office was just four days from january 25 to january 29 1887 this term came at the end of the conservative government of his predecessor john jones ross ross had lost the 1886 quebec election but had tried to cling to power in a minority government for a few more months taillon was leader of the opposition from 1887 until 1890 when he lost the 1890 election and his own seat he briefly returned to the practice of law but following the removal of liberal honor mercier from office by the lieutenant governor of quebec taillon became minister without portfolio in the government of charles eug ne boucher de boucherville taillon became premier when boucher de boucherville resigned he resigned in 1896 and moved into federal politics to serve as postmaster general in the very short lived federal conservative government of charles tupper from may to july 1896 he failed to gain a federal seat in the 1896 federal election and likewise failed to secure a seat in the 1900 federal election ending his political career in 1916 he was made a knight bachelor he died
