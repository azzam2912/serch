o ket un nai means the river at whose mouth there are drying stretching frames for animal skins as of september 2016 the town has an estimated population of 3 042 and a population density of 5 8 persons per km the total area is 527 54 km oketo was originally a logging community and is currently involved in farming as well popular crops include potatoes white flower beans and beets dairy farming and cattle farming are also practiced oketo was the tug of war champion of japan for a number of years in the 1960s that tradition of showing strength continues today in the ningen banba festival which is held in july every year during the ningen bamba teams of 5 or 7 men pull sleds of logs over man made hills to the finish line oketo has one elementary school oketo elementary school previously the town had four active elementary schools but due to the decline in population three of them have been closed oketo junior high school is the only junior high school in town there is also a prefectural high school located in oketo oketo high school which offers a social welfare course of study oketo
