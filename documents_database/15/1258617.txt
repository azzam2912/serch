he completed his phd in physics under gerard j milburn at the university of queensland in 1994 with a thesis entitled quantum trajectories and feedback after his phd wiseman undertook a postdoc under dan walls at the university of auckland from 1996 to 2009 he held australian research council arc research fellowships he is currently a physics professor at griffith university where he is the director of the centre for quantum dynamics he is also an executive node manager in the centre for quantum computation and communication technology an arc centre of excellence his awards include the bragg medal of the australian institute of physics the pawsey medal of the australian academy of science and the malcolm macintosh medal one of the prime minister s prizes for science he is a fellow of the australian academy of science a fellow of the american physical society and a fellow of the optical society of america
