the store was located in the dundas west portugal village but moved in the spring of 2016 to 1267 bloor st west toronto it is well known for its eclectic store front display of arcane and absurd books the monkey s paw was created in 2006 by the owner stephen fowler after moving from san francisco in 2002 where he previously worked in numerous bookstores the impetus for opening the bookstore was due to the need to support his family with another income source the inspiration and book selection methods of the store is based on his work in a previous san francisco bookstore that placed books that were too unique specific or unusual for a single subject category under a miscellaneous section floop many including the owner consider the store itself a showcase and homage to the printed book which they believe to be dying medium due to the notoriety and uniqueness of the bookstore fowler was invited to join the international league of antiquarian booksellers in november 2012 the store introduced a biblio mat machine which dispenses a random book for a 2 coin the new york times s style magazine quoted the owner it s the store
