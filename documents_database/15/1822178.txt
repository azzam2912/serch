a fishing boat carrying illegal migrants hit rocks and sank 61 people died 48 survived the incident the long fishing boat left the village of ahmetbeyli in the menderes district of zmir province at 5 30 am heading for the island of samos to enter greece illegally about off the coast she hit rocks and sank in deep water media reports stated that some people were locked in the hold the district governor said that the migrants were from middle eastern countries and the 61 dead were 31 children including three babies 18 women and eleven men 33 syrians twelve palestinians one iraqi and two turks being the organizer and the captain survived the disaster 46 of the survivors swam ashore while two others were rescued by law enforcement teams the dead bodies were transported to the forensic lab in zmir while the survivors were brought to a hospital in the district for medical checks the two turks were taken into custody
