the band calls its musical style ur folk they re interpret old folk songs of their region and create new songs based on legends stories and the iconic landscape of central switzerland the music consists of elements of traditional volksmusik from switzerland as well as folk music from other regions but also many other different styles of music the project thus follows the old spirit of swiss folk music that has always been a constantly changing and growing mixture of different influences paired with local folklore the lyrics are written and performed in the local high alemannic german dialect since their formation in 2009 fr km ndt have managed to get the attention of an international audience and their albums were positively reviewed in several countries the denomination fr km ndt was the old name of mount pilatus a mysterious mountain range near lucerne fr km ndt was founded in 2009 by k thi chregu and res in 2010 they released a demo tape called losid vo b rge ond tal as well as their debut album urb rglieder the same year hurdy gurdy player anneli anna murphy became a member of the band with the song d draachejongfer fr
