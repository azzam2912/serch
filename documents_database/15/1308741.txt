born in workington cumberland now cumbria his regular position was as an outside left he began his football career with local club black diamonds but joined lancashire side blackburn rovers in 1897 at the age of 20 along with left half peter chambers after three years with blackburn during which time he scored 17 goals in 53 league games and was selected for a football league xi hurst returned to cumberland in 1900 to join his hometown club workington a year later he re entered the football league with manchester city playing 15 times in his season there before joining the newly renamed manchester united in 1902 he made his debut for manchester united away to gainsborough trinity on 6 september 1902 before then scoring in three consecutive games against burton united bristol city and glossop his only other goal for the club came in a 3 1 win over lincoln city on 8 november 1902 he left manchester united at the end of the 1902 03 season before retiring from football hurst married emily borthwick cretney in workington on 18 december 1898 they had three children catherine hurst born c 1899 1900 daniel james hurst jr born c 1902
