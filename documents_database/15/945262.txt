the film is an adventure about the invasion of planet ateneia by intruders trying to steal their energy the planet ateneia located in the constellation of cassiopeia is attacked by space invaders who begin to drain its vital energy a distress signal is sent into outer space by the local astronomer liza and received by four heroes who travel across the galaxy to the rescue the four heroes chip chop feel and thot venture through the galaxy facing many dangers as they try to rescue the planet of ateneia each has a specific function in their spaceship chop is the captain and pilot feel and thot monitor space and chip is the gunman working also as comic relief liza is an astronomer in ateneia s main lab working on all of the scientific details of the planet s life on the way to defeat the evil forces of shadowseat the foursome meet leonardo a scientist from an undeveloped planet who creates crazy gadgets production began in january 1992 with environment and character modeling and the creation of the script animation began in january 1993 and the image generation work was completed on august 1995 the soundtrack was finished in december
