kavallerie division was a unit of the german army in world war i the division was formed on the mobilization of the german army in august 1914 the division was disbanded in 1919 during the demobilization of the german army after world war i it was initially assigned to ii cavalry corps which preceded the 1st and 2nd armies on the western front in november 1914 it was transferred to russia in march 1918 it returned to the western front where it served in alsace until the end of the war it was dismounted in october 1916 and restructured to form the 4th cavalry sch tzen division by the end of the war it was serving under 64th corps z b v armee abteilung b heeresgruppe herzog albrecht von w rttemberg on the western front a more detailed combat chronicle can be found at the german language version of this article on formation in august 1914 the component units of the division were see table of organisation and equipment the 4th cavalry division was extensively reorganised in the course of the war culminating in its conversion to a cavalry sch tzen division that is to say dismounted cavalry here the
