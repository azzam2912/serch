it is based on ernest hemingway s 1937 novel to have and have not but it moves the setting from cuba to the south of iran and the shores of the persian gulf all the events of the film are nationalized it is considered one of the greatest iranian films by critics captain khorshid is a sailor who although only having one hand manages to sail his little boat in his village due to its hot climate and hard living conditions dangerous criminals are sent into exile they want to escape from the area so they ask a middleman to strike a deal with khorshid khorshid is asked to illegally take them out of the country with his boat at first he is reluctant but because of the hardships of living he accepts the job the criminals murder one of the village s wealthiest merchants and steal the money needed for the trip at the beginning of the journey the criminals kill the middleman in the middle of the trip they attack khorshid and his crewman the crewman is killed khorshid faces them single handedly he manages to kill all the criminals but he himself dies due to the injuries
