all 135 seats in the parliament were up for election the parliament of catalonia was the devolved unicameral legislature of the autonomous community of catalonia having legislative power in regional matters as defined by the spanish constitution and the catalan statute of autonomy as well as the ability to vote confidence in or withdraw it from a president of the government voting for the parliament was on the basis of universal suffrage which comprised all nationals over eighteen registered in catalonia and in full enjoyment of their political rights the 135 members of the parliament of catalonia were elected using the d hondt method and a closed list proportional representation with a threshold of 3 percent of valid votes which included blank ballots being applied in each constituency parties not reaching the threshold were not taken into consideration for seat distribution additionally the use of the d hondt method might result in an effective threshold over three percent depending on the district magnitude seats were allocated to constituencies corresponding to the provinces of barcelona gerona l rida and tarragona each constituency was allocated a fixed number of seats 85 for barcelona 17 for gerona 15 for l rida and 18
