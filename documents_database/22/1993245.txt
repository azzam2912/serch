black arab comes to the city of salonica and imposes a daily tax upon its citizens which includes a young woman for his entertainment when the turn comes for doj in s household to pay the tax his sister is to be given to the arab doj in rises from his sickbed after nine years of lying and confronts the arab after killing him he returns to his bed and dies in peace in some variants of the narrative doj in has a wife who attends him together with his sister in most variants there is another villain beside black arab doj in s own blood brother a blacksmith before the duel doj in s sister or wife asks the blacksmith to shoe his horse on the promise to pay later for the service the blacksmith however asks her to sleep with him which she refuses with indignation and tells doj in about the incident doj in having killed the arab visits the blacksmith and cuts his head off the traditional notion that the long illness is a punishment for a grave sin is found in a number of variants in a macedonian version a group of heroes including doj
