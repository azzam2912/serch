1630 seville c 1688 or 1690 was a portuguese baroque composer filipe da madre de deus was born in lisbon about 1630 although he lacked a deep knowledge of music theory he was a skilled composer and vihuelist at the court of philip iv of spain philip iii of portugal until 1640 in 1654 john iv of portugal heard about the notoriety of madre de deus and hired him for the portuguese court two years later john iv died and was succeeded by afonso vi sometime between april 1660 and august 1661 afonso vi nominated madre de deus as mestre da c mara de m sica real master of the royal chamber music in 1668 peter ii was appointed regent for afonso vi his insane brother and all his former employees were taken off the royal payroll thus filipe da madre de deus had to return to spain in 1668 he was succeeded by the renowned ant nio marques l sbio as master of the royal chamber music of portugal madre de deus retired to the monastery of the barefooted mercedarians of san laureano in seville he continued as chapel master of this monastery until at least 1688 the year
