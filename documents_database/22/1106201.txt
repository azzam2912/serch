john doulton married jane duneau a widow from bridgnorth in shropshire who died on 9 april 1841 they had eight children including sir henry mp frederick josiah and alfred in 1815 soon after john doulton had completed his apprenticeship as a potter he invested his life savings of 100 in the vauxhall walk pottery of martha jones lambeth her foreman john watts was also taken into partnership and the firm became known as jones watts and doulton it specialized in industrial ware brown stoneware drain pipes as well as stoneware bottles for chemicals beer and other industrial liquids among others martha jones withdrew from the partnership in 1820 and the company moved to new premises in lambeth high street in 1826 in 1835 john s 15 year old son henry doulton was taken on as an apprentice by 1846 henry had set up an independent lambeth pottery which had become the leader in industrial products particularly sanitation products following the retirement of john watts in 1853 doulton and watts merged with henry s company to become doulton and company and was highly recognised for its lines of hand decorated figurines vases and dinnerware
