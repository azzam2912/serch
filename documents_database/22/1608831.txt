in 1770 he was appointed custos rotulorum of kerry he was created viscount crosbie of ardfert in the county of kerry in 1771 and was even further honoured when he was made earl of glandore in the county of cork in 1776 lord glandore was twice married he married firstly lady theodosia daughter of john bligh 1st earl of darnley in 1745 after her death in may 1777 he married secondly jane daughter of edward vesey and widow of john ward in 1777 he died in april 1781 and was succeeded in the earldom by his only surviving son from his first marriage john the countess of glandore died in september 1787
