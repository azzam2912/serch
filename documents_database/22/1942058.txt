davis is of jamaican and grenadian descent he is a former student of our lady of lourdes r c primary school and cardinal hinsley r c high school he studied b a art design at university of westminster davis began performing live in 2005 on the uk black comedy circuit the following year davis made the final of two national competitions laughing horse and so you think you re funny at the edinburgh fringe festival in 2009 he won fhm stand up hero which was televised on itv4 in 2012 davis debuted his first solo show enter the davism which sold out its run at both the pleasance theatre in edinburgh and london s soho theatre davis is represented by off the kerb and has supported stand up comedians michael mcintyre alan carr and kevin bridges on their respective live tours he has appeared on comedy central s comedy store bbc three s edinburgh comedy fest live bbc two s kevin eldon show mtv s sitcom get rich and the dog ate my homework
