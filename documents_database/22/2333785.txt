translation research is regarded as a broad field encompassing translation interpreting audiovisual translation localization and adaptation the network s agenda appears on its foundation document the document outlines the objectives of a doctoral program in translation studies and the activities required so that students in these programs can acquire the necessary competences its aims include the following promoting the cooperation between existing doctoral programs developing a database of online course materials such as model syllabi presentations and video lectures providing training for supervisors compiling a list of translation scholars willing to supervise and co supervise phds in their fields of expertise facilitating joint doctorates especially those involving different countries assisting students whose research work necessitates travelling to other countries organizing id ts students conferences tightening the collaboration with the industry and governmental and non governmental institutions raising external funding for the activities of the network in 2018 there are 14 members which represent doctoral programs from 11 countries aston university britain bar ilan university israel bo azi i university turkey charles university prague czech republic jagiellonian university krakow poland ku leuven belgium university of antwerp belgium university of bologna forl italy university of geneva switzerland university of leeds britain university
