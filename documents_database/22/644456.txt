w ll ri led the finnish seamen s union from 1938 until his death in 1913 w ll ri left finland to become seaman in 1916 he settled in the united states and joined the industrial workers of the world because of his political activities w ll ri was deported back to finland in 1920 w ll ri became active in the underground communist party of finland skp and the public socialist workers party of finland sstp w ll ri was chairman of sstp in 1922 1923 and he was jailed at the tammisaari prison camp when the party was disbanded in the late 1920s w ll ri became frustrated with communists this eventually led to a split in 1929 w ll ri was one of the main organisers behind the short lived left group of finnish workers w ll ri joined the social democratic party of finland in 1935 social democratic association of transport workers was his stronghold the soviet union radioed anti w ll ri propaganda during the second world war after the war w ll ri left sdp and joined the finnish people s democratic league and the socialist unity party he became famous as the leader
