the russian position from the front was accessible only by three dams which passed through the marshy tributary of the mukhavets river its right flank rested against the swamp the left was covered by a forest considered impassable through which the road to kobryn passed prince schwarzenberg sent a detachment bypassing the left flank the unexpected appearance of which caused tormasov to draw almost all the troops there all enemy attacks on the russian left flank were repulsed and nothing decisive was attempted against the russian front thanks to this tormasov despite the great superiority of forces from the enemy side stayed on the position until the evening at night fearing for his retreat he went to kobryn
