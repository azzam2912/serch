the modern wind power industry began in 1979 with the serial production of wind turbines by danish manufacturers the industry is currently undergoing a period of rapid globalization and consolidation wind farm group of wind turbines in the same location used to produce electric power a large wind farm may consist of several hundred individual wind turbines and cover an extended area of hundreds of square miles but the land between the turbines may be used for agricultural or other purposes a wind farm may also be located offshore wind turbine a turbine that converts wind energy into mechanical energy wind powered vehicle typically uses a sail to harness the wind to propel it also includes kite driven vehicles the predominant type of wind powered vehicles are seafaring vessels sailboats and yachts
