he was a student at the university of tulsa in 1951 1952 then was conscripted into the u s army and was stationed in texas where he played in a military band in 1954 he moved to los angeles and began playing at the lighthouse and with terry gibbs june christy and lena horne in 1961 he moved to new york city and enrolled at the manhattan school of music graduating with a degree in music in 1966 following this he worked with al cohn benny goodman jim hall morgana king lee konitz peggy lee liza minnelli george mraz mark murphy the national jazz ensemble ken peplowski tom pierson zoot sims carol sloane and warren vach jr he played piano in the broadway production of cats from 1982 to 2000 after this he moved to france where he remained until his death in 2015
