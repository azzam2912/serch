charles d amboise acted as governor of paris of the duchy of milan of the seignory of genoa and of the province of normandie in 1501 he was made french lieutenant general and then vice roy for lombardy becoming a friend of leonardo da vinci during his stay in milan he was grand master of france from 1502 to 1504 when he became marshal of france from 1508 to 1510 he was also admiral of france and in 1507 suppressed a revolt in genoa at the battle of agnadello 1509 he commanded the french vanguard in 1510 he took command of the french forces fighting against pope julius ii in the romagna for which he was excommunicated he failed to prevent julius from capturing bologna and mirandola and died of an illness at correggio during the 1511 campaign his son george died in the battle of pavia 1525
