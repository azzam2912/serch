dupuis cmm cstj cd qhp cspq frcpc born 21 november 1928 was the 28th canadian surgeon general born in drummondville qu bec robert was educated at laval university in qu bec city where he graduated with a medical degree in 1951 he also completed post graduate studies in 1961 1964 and 1965 at the montr al military hospital queen mary s hospital and the notre dame hospital both in montr al qu bec dupuis joined the royal canadian army medical corps rcamc in 1956 and was posted to 3rd battalion the canadian guards and was sent to germany in 1957 he returned to canada in 1961 and began post graduate studies however in 1963 he halted his studies and went on a 6 month tour as medical officer with the 57th canadian signal unit in the congo an experience that he greatly enjoyed following his return to canada in 1964 he continued his post graduate studies completing them in 1965 at which time he was posted to the canadian forces hospital in kingston ontario as assistant chief of medicine he remained in the position until 1966 when he was posted to the military hospital in gatineau qu bec he was
