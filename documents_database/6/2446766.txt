not to be confused by their 1961 debut album the soul of ike tina turner this album was released by kent records in 1966 after four years with sue records ike and tina signed with kent records in 1964 on the kent label they released their first live album ike tina turner revue live and a handful of singles the track good bye so long was released as single on modern records it reached number 34 on the billboard s r b singles chart and number 107 on bubbling under the hot 100 the second single i don t need also released on modern reached number 134 on bubbling under the hot 100 tina made a solo appearance on shindig to promote the single in august 1965 she also performed the b side gonna have fun on hollywood a go go the next month a third single i wish my dream would come true was released by kent in 1967 the soul of ike tina was reissued on cd by p vine records with 13 additional tracks
