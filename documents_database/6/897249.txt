some of the scenes near the beginning of the film were shot at brooklyn college in flatbush brooklyn the film s title track was recorded by the american band the fleshtones and the subsequent video was given rotation on mtv other bands and artists appearing on the film s soundtrack include the del fuegos the db s dream syndicate the violent femmes the waitresses the smithereens los lobos alex chilton and the ben vaughn group the original soundtrack record is out of print writing in the zombie movie encyclopedia academic peter dendle called it an irreverent amateur parody of high school romance films in the sixteen candles tradition dendle cited the film as one of the forebears of the zombie romantic comedy trend of the 1980s and 1990s
