mach gogogo was originally serialized in print in shueisha s 1966 sh nen book it was released in tank bon book form by sun wide comics and later re released in japan by fusosha adapted into anime by tatsunoko productions its 52 episodes aired on fuji tv from april 1967 to march 1968 the anime was later re broadcast on tokyo mx from july 1 to september 25 2008 selected chapters of the manga were released by now comics in the 1990s under the title speed racer classics these were later released by wildstorm productions a division of dc comics as speed racer the original manga in 2008 under its americanized title speed racer mach gogogo was republished in its entirety in the united states by digital manga publishing and was released as a box set to commemorate the franchise s 40th anniversary as well as serving as a tie in with the 2008 film the television series itself is an early example of an anime becoming a successful franchise in the united states spawning multiple spinoffs in both print and broadcast media mach gogogo was first created and designed by anime pioneer tatsuo yoshida 1932 1977 as a manga
