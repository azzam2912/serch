in early april 1902 under the command of commander l f g tippinge she left bermuda for home waters calling at faial island before she arrived at devonport on 20 april she was paid off at chatham on 13 may 1902 in 1904 she was converted to a drill ship for the royal naval volunteer reserve at blackfriars london and in 1911 buzzard relieved hms president formerly of 1878 as headquarters ship being renamed hms president on 1 april 1911 as president she served until 23 january 1918 when she was lent to the marine society she was sold to c a beard for breaking on 6 september 1921 and was later re sold to dutch ship breakers
