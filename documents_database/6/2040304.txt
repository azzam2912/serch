a total of 25 teams competed in the qualifying play off to decide eight of the 32 places in the group stage of the 2015 afc champions league the bracket for the qualifying play off which consisted of three rounds preliminary round 1 preliminary round 2 and play off round was determined by the afc based on the association ranking of each team each tie was played as a single match with the team from the higher ranked association hosting the match extra time and penalty shoot out were used to decide the winner if necessary the winners of each tie in the play off round advanced to the group stage to join the 24 automatic qualifiers all losers in each round which were from associations with only play off slots entered the afc cup group stage the following 25 teams 11 from west zone 14 from east zone were involved in the qualifying play off the schedule of the competition was as follows al ahli advanced to group d naft tehran advanced to group b bunyodkor advanced to group a al sadd advanced to group c fc seoul advanced to group h kashiwa reysol advanced to group e guangzhou
