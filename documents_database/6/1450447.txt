the m5s was founded on 4 october 2009 by beppe grillo a comedian and blogger and gianroberto casaleggio a web strategist in 2014 grillo appointed a directorate composed of five leading mps alessandro di battista luigi di maio roberto fico carla ruocco and carlo sibilia which lasted until the following october when he dissolved it and proclaimed himself the political head of the m5s grillo is also formally president of the association named the five star movement his nephew enrico grillo serves as vice president and his accountant enrico maria nadasi as secretary davide casaleggio gianroberto s son has an increasingly important albeit unofficial role the m5s is variously considered populist anti establishment environmentalist anti globalist and eurosceptic the party has also been described as new right and described by some as being right wing due to its anti immigration stance despite its promotion of policies usually advocated by the italian left wing such as citizen s income and green inspired policies grillo himself once provocatively referred to the movement as populist its members stress that the m5s is not a party but a movement and it may not be included in the traditional left right paradigm the five stars
