she was a packet ship in robert kermit s red star line the ship was named after a kermit investor new york merchant stephen whitney the 1 034 ton ship left new york city on 18 october for liverpool carrying passengers and a cargo which included corn raw cotton cheese resin and 20 boxes of clocks on 10 november in thick fog the captain c w popham mistook the crookhaven lighthouse for the one at the old head of kinsale and the lighthouse on cape clear island was obscured by fog compounding the error in navigation at around 10 pm the ship struck the western tip of west calf island completely breaking up within about ten minutes the loss of the ship triggered the decision to replace the cape clear island lighthouse with one on fastnet rock this decision was also because the lighthouse on cape clear was often shrouded in fog or low level cloud which made it hard to see and at time impossible to see
