mandzhukov was a brother of the colonel of the bulgarian army spas mandzhukov he was a nephew is metropolitan nathanael of ohrid who lead him to plovdiv eastern rumelia where he studied at a high school mandzhukov became one of the founders of the anarchist macedonian secret revolutionary committee there and was therefore excluded from the school he graduated later from a pedagogical school in lom and afterwards studied chemistry at the university of geneva where he became a member of the anarchist geneva group in the autumn of 1898 he returned to ottoman macedonia and became a member of the imaro sentenced in march 1899 to a life prison for a conspiracy mandzhukov appealed the verdict and in the same year the court dismissed him for failing to prosecute subsequently he entered the cheta of gotse delchev in 1899 in 1900 he resided in thessaloniki where had a contact with the gemidzii and they were influenced by his anarchist ideas especially those relating to methods of struggle in 1900 together with pavel shatev slavi merdjanov and petar sokolov he took part in the terrorist activity around the ottoman bank in istanbul after their arrest and release mandzhukov emigrated to
