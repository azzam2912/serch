the brass plate which covered the foundation stone cavity was inscribed with hunc lapidam dis sacr st thom dedicate in memoriam edward t cox et joannae conjugis posuit isabella sands cox kal nov xxviii a s mdccclxx t bateman et corser arch t hardwick et fil dif it was consecrated on 14 august 1883 by the bishop of worcester in 1884 a parish was assigned with land taken from the parish of st paul s church balsall heath part of the parish was taken in 1900 to form part of the new parish of st patrick s church bordesley when the church was closed the parish was split part merged with st mary and st ambrose edgbaston and the rest with st anne s church moseley the church contained an organ by nicholson of worcester a specification of the organ can be found on the national pipe organ register when the church was closed the organ was moved to st john the baptist s church longbridge
