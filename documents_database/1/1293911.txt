sumner was cast in all five of the company s productions for which casting information exists he played these roles he probably also played in the company s production of john ford s love s sacrifice as well the roles he played tended to be young male leads and vigorous dashing figures which reveals something about the kind of actor he was james wright s historia histrionica 1699 states that sumner was a roommate of richard perkins fellow actor with the queen s company as wright put it perkins and sumner of the cockpit theatre kept house together in clerkenwell and were there buried the date of sumner s death is not known although he was buried on 24 may 1649
