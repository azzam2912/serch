in june 1635 christian iv of denmark norway in his vessel which lay at anchor at flekker y harbor ordered construction of a naval base and christians fortress on small slottsholmen island now called gammel en or old island the island commands the western approaches to flekker y harbor a port long frequented by ships of many nations the town of kristiansand was also soon founded there in 1641 to strengthen control of the area flekker y lies off the coast of the population center at kristiansand and is a part of kristiansand municipality christian iv again visited flekker y in 1644 during the hannibal war while the dutch english war 1652 1654 was being fought j rgen bjelke was named lord of the region and commander of christians fortress to strengthen the defenses in the event that oliver cromwell s forces should attempt to seize it to prey on the extensive dutch norwegian trade bjelke was later to play a major role in the northern war construction on the fortress was never completed when the danger from cromwell ceased this fort was abandoned and replaced by the more strategically located fredriksholm fortress which commanded both approaches to the harbor
