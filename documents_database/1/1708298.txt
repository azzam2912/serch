the remaining floor crossing provisions were repealed by the fourteenth amendment which was enacted at the same time the fourteenth amendment contained the repeal provisions which affected the provincial legislatures and the national council of provinces ncop and therefore had to be approved by six of nine provinces in the ncop as well as by two thirds of the national assembly while the fifteenth amendment contained the remaining provisions which only had to be approved by the assembly the bills for both amendments were passed by the national assembly on 20 august 2008 with the fifteenth amendment receiving 309 votes in favour and none opposed both amendments were signed by president kgalema motlanthe on 6 january 2009 and came into force on 17 april 2009 days before the 2009 general election
