he was given command of the fourth rate hms winchester in 1842 and the flagship hms victory in 1847 from 1848 to 1852 he acted as private secretary to george eden 1st earl of auckland and sir francis baring 3rd baronet successively first lords of the admiralty he succeeded his cousin henry eden to that position he took command of the second rate hms london in 1853 and saw action in the bombardment of sevastopol during the crimean war he was awarded the order of the medjidie 3rd class in 1858 he then became comptroller general of the coastguard in 1855 third naval lord in 1859 and second naval lord in 1861 he was promoted to rear admiral in 1861 and to vice admiral in 1866 he was made a companion of the order of the bath cb in 1863 he was placed on the retired list in 1870 and elevated to knight commander of the order of the bath kcb in the 1873 birthday honours he died in spring gardens westminster in 1878 in 1828 he married emma williams and in 1866 he married fanny cecilia grenville there were no children from either marriage
